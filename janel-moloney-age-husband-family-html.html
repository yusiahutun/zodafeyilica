    American actress Janel Moloney is a popular face on TV. She shot to fame as Donna Moss in the TV show, The West Wing (1999-2006). Since then, she has appeared in a number of TV shows namely Law & Order: Criminal Intent (2009), The Good Wife (2003), and The Blacklist (2015). 

    In 2020, she is set to appear as Sally Morrison in the upcoming TV show, The Undoing. 

    Married Status Now

    Janel Moloney is married to Marcelo Zarvos since 5 January 2010. The pair choose to keep their marital life a low-key affair. 

    Janel Moloney with husband Marcelo Zarvos and son Julian Francis Zarvos on 20 June 2010 (Photo: gettyimages.com)

    However, it is a piece of common knowledge that the pair are parents to two children. Janel’s sons are Julian Francis Zarvos (born on 14 February 2010) and Fernando Luca Zarvos (born on 26 February 2012). 

    Who Is Janel’s Husband? 

    Marcelo Zarvos is a Brazilian pianist and composer. The São Paulo native is known for his work in movies namely Enough Said (2013), Fences (2016) and Wonder (2017).

    Family Info 

    Janel was born and brought up in Woodland Hills, California. She is the youngest child of parents Judy Moloney (mother) and John Moloney (father). Her older siblings are Lance Moloney, Meegan Moloney, and Carey Moloney. Carey is Janel’s twin who is just eight-minute older than the actress.

    Movie & TV Shows

    Janel started her showbiz career with the TV show, Roomies (1997). She followed her debut with TV movies like To Save a Child (1991), ...And Then She Was Gone (1991), and Double Edge (1992). Further, she was also part of the 1993 movie titled Dream Lover.
