    Her slurred speech and unusual behaviour has raised more than a few eyebrows on the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

    But her co-star and plastic surgeon Dr. Paul Nassif has revealed it is the result of prescription medication.

    During a consultation with Adrienne Maloof's husband which aired last night, the 46-year-old said she has been taking a cocktail of drugs under the advice of her doctor.

    On medication: Kim Richards revealed she takes prescription drugs during last night's episode of Real Housewives of Beverly Hills

    She is on anti-anxiety medication Lexapro, the antidepressant Trazadone, and Topamax, an anti-seizure medication which can be used to treat alcoholism.

    'I'm glad you told me this, because now it all makes sense,' Nassif, who had previously expressed concern about her bizarre antics, was seen telling her.

    '[The combination] is going to make you slur the words a little bit. It's going to make you out of it.'

    'Out of it': Adrienne Maloof's plastic surgeon husband Dr. Paul Nassif said the 'damn medication' was to blame for Kim's behaviour

    'You have that sedated look on you right now,' he added.

    'This is why everyone's going, you know,"'Is she on something right now?"' Nassif added. 'It's the damn medication.'

    Earlier this season, co-star Brandi Glanville accused her of using crystal meth - which she denied.

    Kim's sister Kyle Richards accused her of being an alcoholic in last year's finale. She is understood to have sought treatment after the dramatic showdown.

    'I am afraid that people think I'm drinking,' Kim admitted last night's show, insisting she hadn't had any alcohol 'for a while.'

    Not tears of joy: Kyle cried as Kim told her she had been in a secret relationship for a year

    Not seeing eye-to-eye: Kim and Kyle rarely agree on how her older sister should to live her life

    The former child actress' new romance was also a hot topic on the episode.

    Kim finally told her younger sister that she been secretly dating someone for an entire year - and is moving in with him.

    A shocked and tearful Kyle then guessed it's Ken, Kim's date to the premiere for their niece, Paris Hilton's new reality show, The World According to Paris earlier this year.

    Kim's mysterious new man is said to be Ken Blumenfeld, who is described in reports as a financial adviser from Westlake, California.

    He is understood to have children and as Kyle pointed out in preview for the next episode, he still wears a wedding ring.
