    Kelly Osbourne's adding to her collection of body art!

    The "Fashion Police" star got a new tattoo over the weekend ON HER HEAD, and posted a few snaps of her ink via Instagram.

    Osbourne's new tat reads "Stories ...," which is written in an old school typewriter font.

    In one shot, Kelly poses with the tattoo artist with her new piece fully on display. "Thank you @dr_woo_ssc it's magic what you can do! @shamrocksocialclub," she captioned the candid snap.

    In another shot, the reality star's pictured lying down with her head on a chair as she gets the ink engraved on the side of her shaved 'do.

    "First female member of the #ShamrockCrew thank you @Dr_woo_ssc for the head tattoo!," she posted with the pic.
