    Jessica McCormack and Olivia von Halle team up on collection

    28 May 2024, 00:00

    It is easy to take for granted the craftsmanship that goes into making some of the world's most beautiful luxury products, which makes it all the more fascinating when you have the opportunity to see the process for yourself. Our new franchise, Inside The Atelier, celebrates exactly this. For our latest iteration, we are looking at the beautiful collaboration between luxury nightwear label Olivia von Halle and London jeweller Jessica McCormack, which includes a very special pair of pyjamas, complete with diamond and ruby buttons.

    Created in celebration of wedding season, the collaboration sees the two designers (who happen to be great friends) team up for the very first time on a romance-infused collection of pyjamas, robes and eye masks, created in sumptuous silks and adorned with beautiful embroidery. On one very special pair of pyjamas – which are made-to-order – the buttons feature rubies and diamonds, which have been handcrafted into buttons in the Jessica McCormack workshop.

    Courtesy of Olivia von Halle x Jessica McCormack

    Courtesy of Olivia von Halle x Jessica McCormack

    “We know each other’s brands really well, and I think quite quickly we knew we would use some of my signature motifs as a starting point,” McCormack told us. “Liv and her team took those embroidered within our Heirloom Jewellery Boxes, and from there created pretty prints for the pyjamas. Naturally I wanted to find a way to incorporate diamonds. So adding ruby and diamond buttons to the made-to-order pieces was the perfect finishing touch. I love the duality of something really opulent paired with something casual. Who wouldn’t want to lounge around in a silk pyjama covered in diamonds?”

    Courtesy of Olivia von Halle x Jessica McCormack

    Related Story

    Named the Yves Desire Pyjama, the set can be ordered with the gemstone buttons (which are detachable), while every piece in the collection can also be personalised with hand-embroidered lettering, making them particularly special for a bride to be or her bridesmaids.

    “Although we work in different categories, we share a lot of the same clients, and we have the same passion for craftsmanship,” McCormack adds. “We have a mutual understanding of the woman that buys our pieces, and what she wants – and that’s been fundamental for both our brands. I think you can really see that in what we’ve created together.”

    Courtesy of Olivia von Halle x Jessica McCormack

    Courtesy of Olivia von Halle x Jessica McCormack

    The craftsmanship of these pieces is truly something to be celebrated, and that’s before you even add the diamonds.

    “Our pyjamas are a real labour of love, from the very early development stages until the moment they land in store,” explains von Halle. “I'm an absolute stickler for detail and we can spend hours, days, sometimes months honing the tiniest detail on a product before it goes to market.”

    Courtesy of Olivia von Halle x Jessica McCormack

    “The star of this collaboration is of course the Yves Desire pyjama, easily our most opulent creation to date. It has one 0.34ct heart-shaped ruby button and four 0.30ct heart-shaped diamond buttons. Each button is made-to-order in Jessica McCormack’s Mayfair Townhouse, and the edges have been cut in such a way as to allow the optimum amount of light and sparkle. The same piece is finished with beautiful embroidery on the shirt using a technique which dates back to the 12th Century. Literally the pyjama of dreams.”

    The collection is available to shop from today. Prices start at £150 for an eye mask, while the made-to-order silk pyjamas go up to £16,650. You can purchase them here.
