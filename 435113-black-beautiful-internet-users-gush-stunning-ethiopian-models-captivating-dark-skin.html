    Can never bleach her skin

    Four years ago, the 28-year-old revealed that an Uber driver had asked her if she would ever consider bleaching her skin.

    "Don’t take this offensively but if you were given 10,000 dollars, would you bleach your skin for that amount?" she wrote on Instagram, as reported by the Indian Express.

    According to the dark-skinned model, she did not even respond but simply laughed it off so hard, sending the message that it was impossible.

    "Why on earth would I ever bleach this beautiful melanin God blessed me with?" she added, making the driver amazed that she viewed the skin tone as a blessing.

    She added that she has gotten used to receiving such looks and weird questions about her skin every time she goes out in the public.
