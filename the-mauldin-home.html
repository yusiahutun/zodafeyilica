    A fifth generation Laurelite is ready to make Laurel his home again and we couldn't be any more excited to be apart of his next adventure! 

    Shuff Mauldin is an Ole Miss grad, avid traveler, and insurance agent here in Laurel. He is a young entrepreneur who is ready to show those looking at Laurel, that this place can be so much more than just a retirement community. It has life and an abundance of things to offer someone who is young, single, and working in this little town. 

    He needed a space that was the perfect size for himself and for accommodating guests, a large kitchen for entertaining, and a place that would be a great investment as his life grows and changes. This cozy cottage in the middle of Laurel's beautiful historic district was exactly what he was looking for! Traveling is a huge part of who he is and what he enjoys, so a forever home wasn't on the brain. A home that would be a great choice for his current walk of life and as support for his hometown was the goal. 

    "People need to see Laurel has single entrepreneurs who choose to call it home. I love to travel and this is one of the best places to do it from. Because of my low cost of living I can travel the world well before retirement. This place is hip and people need to know it."

    This beautiful live edge custom coffee table from Ben was just the piece to add effortless character to this bachelor's paradise. 

    Shuff, now you can kick up your feet on that brand new front porch, bring friends and family over, and have the coziest space to come home to after your travels. We're really glad you're back in the neighborhood!
