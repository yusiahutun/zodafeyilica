    A few years ago, I bought a pair of underwear I loved so much, I’d run my washing machine superfluously, solely so I could wear them more frequently. In recent years, I’ve come to my senses and purchased another pair of my beloved undies: the Cosabella Never Say Never Hottie Boyshort from Italian-made lingerie brand Cosabella. Impressed by the boyshort style, I’ve also ventured into its thong category — which has proven similarly stellar. Luckily for me, both styles are marked down during Cosabella’s Seasonal Sale, as are additional bras, underwear, and sleepwear, up to 60 percent off.

    The Cosabella Never Say Never Hottie Boyshort is a pair of cheeky-cut underwear made with flattery at the forefront of its design. Available in myriad sizes, including an extended range up to 4X, the panties are a brand best-seller — and an unrivaled favorite in my underwear collection.

    If the thought of lace underwear incites itchiness, rest assured: Cosabellas’ defy the notion that lace-trim underwear is uncomfortable. In fact, the boyshorts are among the most comfortable pairs I own. The secret behind non-scratchy lace? A wide, large-format version, made from a polyamide and elastane blend. 

    This material also allows for ample stretch — yet not too much stretch: You won’t experience sagging, slipping, or gapping between the lower back and rear. Cosabella’s undies lay perfectly flat against my skin and conform to my curves as if they were created for my body. The gusset, primarily made of cotton, gives the underwear a breathable feel that’s comfortable even in sweaty seasons. 

    Comfort aside, the Never Say Never Boy Short is fantastically flattering despite its low-rise cut. Typically, such a descriptor causes me to recoil, yet these hip-skimming panties prove that there is, in fact, a place for low-rise styles in this world. (For anyone who disagrees: Shop the boyshort in its high-rise rendition.) The boyshorts are available in 36 colors, from racy red to pretty periwinkle to — my favorite — a cobalt so vibrant, it’s almost otherworldly. 

    Like the boyshort, the Never Say Never Thong is available in a rainbow of options. Incidentally, many of the brand’s corresponding bralettes — the likes of which Ariana Grande has worn — are on sale and pair perfectly with any bottoms from the Never Say Never collection. (Grande’s exact bandeau has been discontinued, but you can still snag similar styles.)

    Care to upgrade your underwear assortment with ultra-flattering, super-comfortable Italian lingerie? Shop Cosabella’s sitewide sale while you still can. Hurry, deals end soon.
