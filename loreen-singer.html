    About

    Loreen is a 40 year old Swedish Singer. Born Lorine Zineb Nora Talhaoui on 16th October, 1983 in Stockholm, Stockholms län, Sweden, she is famous for her appearance in the inaugural season of the TV4 reality-competition talent series Swedish Idol in 2004, under the name Lorén Talhaoui, and for winning the Eurovision Song Contest 2012 in Baku, with the song "Euphoria", and the Eurovision Song Contest 2023 in Liverpool, with the song "Tattoo", in addition to her discography consisting of several tracks, extended plays, and albums such as Heal, My Heart Is Refusing Me, Ride, Under ytan, Nude, Sober, Crying Out Your Name, We Got the Power, In My Head, I’m in It with You, Body, Paper Light (Higher), We Got the Power, Hate the Way I Love You, Fiction Feels Good, Sötvattentårar, Walk with Me, and Neon Lights in a career that spans 2004–present.     Lorine Zineb Nora Talhaoui (born 16 October 1983), known professionally as Loreen (), is a Swedish singer and songwriter. Representing Sweden, she has won the Eurovision Song Contest twice – in 2012 and 2023 – with the songs "Euphoria" and "Tattoo". She is the second performer (after Johnny Logan) and the first and only woman to have won the competition twice to date.
