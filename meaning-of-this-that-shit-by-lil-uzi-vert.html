    Meaning of This That Shit by Lil Uzi Vert

    02 Mar 2024, 00:00

    In Lil Uzi Vert's song titled 'This That Shit,' the overarching theme revolves around the pursuit of success, fame, and obtaining wealth. The lyrics express an unapologetic and audacious attitude towards achieving these goals. While the explicit content and language may be divisive, a deeper analysis reveals several unexpected and imaginative themes hidden within the song's lyrics.One standout lyric that contributes to the development of the song's theme is "I can fuck your bitch, put my dick up in her stomach." While this line initially appears vulgar and provocative, it can be seen as a metaphor for the artist's desire to penetrate and leave a lasting impact on the music industry. The stomach symbolizes the core, the essence, and the depth of one's artistry, indicating that Lil Uzi Vert aims to leave a profound impression with his music.Another interesting lyric is "I fantasize about the bitches working at Onyx, now them same bitches on my dick, tryna get my commas." This line explores the idea of transformation and the evolution of one's social status. It highlights how Lil Uzi Vert's rise to fame and wealth has led to a reversal of roles. The women he once desired are now pursuing him, hoping to gain access to his material success. This lyric brings attention to the desire for recognition and the subsequent change in interpersonal dynamics that success can bring.The line "That beat go so hard my god give me concussion" ties into the theme of determination and persistence. The metaphor emphasizes the power of the music itself, suggesting that the strength and impact of one's work can become physically overwhelming. Lil Uzi Vert's dedication to his craft pushes him to endure the challenges and hardships that accompany his journey towards success.Additionally, the lyric "Ring on my finger different color like the diseases" delves into the juxtaposition of luxurious symbols and the severity of societal issues. While diamonds typically symbolize wealth and opulence, the comparison to diseases highlights the darker side of the world, drawing attention to the various challenges and inequalities that coexist. This lyric showcases Lil Uzi Vert's awareness of the world's complexities, as he navigates through both material success and the harsh realities of life.As a whole, 'This That Shit' encapsulates the pursuit of success, fame, and material wealth, which are common themes in contemporary hip-hop. However, by dissecting the standout lyrics, we uncover unexpected and imaginative themes such as the desire for artistic impact, the transformation of social dynamics, the power of music, and a reminder of the world's complexities. These additional themes enhance the depth and multi-dimensional nature of the song, inviting listeners to reflect on their own ambitions and the consequences that come with achieving them.
