    Rafael Chaouiche's on Making the Cut 

    The show pits designers worldwide with the opportunity to design and create the next big trendy fashion design. Judges then test design themes, mettle capacity, talent, technique, and attention to detail to choose a winner.

    Winners then receive a $1 million reward to invest in their business and become the next sensation in the world of fashion. In Making the Cut, contestants were asked to present ten high-fashion runway outfits in under four weeks.

    The ten initial contestants would then be accessed and graded by competing amongst themselves. The contestants were also required to formulate solid business plans to pitch to Amazon's Christine Beauchamp. 

    Representing Brazil, Rafael ended as a runner-up in the Amazon Prime reality show, losing to winner Yannik Zamboni. Zamboni, from Swiss, caught the eye of judges and audiences with his traditional menswear design, which was inspired by the gender-neutral approach.
