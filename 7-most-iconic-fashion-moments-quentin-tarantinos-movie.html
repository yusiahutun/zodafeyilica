    7 most iconic fashion moments in Quentin Tarantino’s movie Once Upon a Time in Hollywood

    The hotly anticipated Quentin Tarantino film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood is already a success at the box office. The star-stacked cast features Leonardo DiCaprio, Brad Pitt and Margot Robbie as the first three names on the call sheet, not to mention familiar faces such as Dakota Fanning.

    The movie’s story centres on Rick Dalton, a washed-up actor with a fading career played by Leonardo DiCaprio, and his partner-in-crime stuntman and chauffeur, Cliff Booth, played by Brad Pitt. Margot Robbie is cast as Sharon Tate, a popular model and actress in the 1960s who was murdered by the Manson family. In true Tarantino fashion, the movie is filled with satire and violence, while somehow still looking glamorous.

    Is it the end of the Victoria’s Secret fashion show?

    The film has received mixed reviews, with some fans fawning over the Brad-Leo bromance and others dissatisfied with it being a little less conventionally Tarantino.
