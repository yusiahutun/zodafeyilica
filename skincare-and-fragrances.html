    Bath time just got much more interesting!

      There is nothing quite like a long hot bath, particularly at this time of the year when it's freezing cold outside. Everyone knows how amazing bath bombs are when you need an indulgent soak in the tub, and LUSH is the first place we head to when we need to stock up. So you can imagine our delight when we heard the news that the brand has produced a bath bomb that's bigger and better than ever!

      The Giant Golden Bath Bomb weighs two and a half pounds! 

      The Giant Golden Bath Bomb is absolutely enormous and is a whopping SIX times the size of its regular counterpart! It weighs over two and a half pounds, and is the ultimate festive treat. Shaped like an enormous Christmas gift, it includes 27 sumptuous ingredients to totally invigorate and uplift you, including orange, lime and cognac oil. Don't be deceived by its golden appearance; when you drop the brick-like present into hot water, it wizzes and sparkles, and turns your bath water a deep, bold turquoise! It will also make your bathroom smell sweet and fruity, which is a must for bath time fun.

      Its a huge six times the size of the regular Golden Wonder offering! 

      Priced at £12.95, it's understandably a little more than the standard size, which retails at £4.50, but considering the quantity you get, it's totally justified. A fabulous stocking filler for someone special, or even a treat for yourself - it is Christmas after all! This particular bath bomb is only available at Christmas time, so we recommend that you stock up while you can…
