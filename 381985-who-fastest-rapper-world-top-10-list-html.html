There are numerous Hip-Hop artists worldwide, most of whom are a force to reckon with in their country or globally. However, some rappers have stood out as having faster rapping speeds.     Carl Terrell Mitchell, popularly known as Twista, was born on November 27, 1973, in Chicago, Illinois, United States.     Several individuals have described Twista as the fastest rapper in the world. He held the Guinness World Record for Fastest Rap MC in 1992 after rapping 598 syllables in 55 seconds, which translated to an average of 11.2 syllables per second. However, the musician has released numerous tracks since then, showing off his impressive skills.

    2. Eminem

    Marshall Bruce Mathers III, also known as Eminem, was born on October 17, 1972, in Saint Joseph, Missouri, United States. He is an actor, rapper and music producer.
