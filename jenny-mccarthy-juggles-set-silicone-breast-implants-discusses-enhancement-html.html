    Jenny McCarthy showed off a unique talent during a Thursday taping of her talk show as she toyed with a set of silicone breast implants.

    The bubbly blonde couldn't resist juggling the pair before speaking about her own procedures with plastic surgeon Dr. Terry Dubrow.

    The former Playboy model, 40, underwent her first surgical enhancement at age 19.
