    Sauti Sol Announce They'll Take Indefinite Break from Band Activities after US, Europe Tour

    Sauti Sol has announced they are taking a break after 20 years together. Sauti Sol, via their social media accounts, broke their fan's hearts with their announcement. The group noted it was taking an indefinite hiatus. They will go on tour before the break.

    28 Jun 2024, 00:00
    • Sauti Sol, a group made up of four talented artistes announced they are going on an indefinite hiatus after 20 years together as a band
    • The group, which comprises; Bien Aimé Baraza, Chimano Willis, Polycarp Otieno, and Savara Mudigi, are loved by their fans
    • The group will do their final tour together in US and Europe, excluding Kenya and Africa before their split

    Sauti Sol has announced they are taking a break after 20 years together.

    Sauti Sol on an indefinite hiatus

    Sauti Sol, via their social media accounts, broke their fan's hearts with their announcement.

    The group noted it was taking an indefinite hiatus. They will go on tour before the break.

    The band will tour the US and Europe, and fans noted the band excluded the motherland Kenya.

    Bensoul on leaving Sol Generation

    In April, Bensoul who was signed to Sol Generation, talked about him leaving the label.

    In an interview with Mungai Eve, the singer noted that leaving Sol was for him to grow as a man, but he still had much love for the members.

    "According to my contract with them, it was supposed to end this year. It has been nearly four years and some months. They have built me. Right now, I have changed. Since the contract has ended, I feel like its time I did my own thing. I want to build my own legacy. In Sol I want to leave some space for some artistes to find their feet.
