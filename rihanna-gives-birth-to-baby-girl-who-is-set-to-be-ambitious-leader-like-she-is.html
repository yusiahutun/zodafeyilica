    10 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Rihanna has reportedly given birth a baby girl, her first daughter with partner A$AP Rocky, outlets report. The star announced her second pregnancy during this year’s Superbowl Half Time performance, less than a year after giving birth to her first son, RZA.

    The music stars kept their due date on the down low, although it was clear she was over the three-month mark when the epic public announcement was made. Now, reports are coming through the singer has given birth to her second child, with the bundle of joy being a girl.

    Rihanna reportedly ‘given birth to a baby girl’

    As per Media Takeout, Rihanna has given birth to a baby girl, with partner A$AP Rocky, multiple sources have told the outlet, they report.

    One source, a friend of the couple, reportedly described the little girl as “precious” and “beautiful”. Another claimed: “She’s a perfect spitting image of Rihanna – even down to the light eyes.”

    The reported arrival comes just days after the release of the mogul’s Savage x Fenty Maternity line. The celebrity star has often posed in her clothing line while proudly showing off her bump. Instagram fans have enjoyed seeing her glowing while pregnant.

    Rihanna or ASAP Rocky have not yet confirmed the news.

    GRV Media and Celebrity Tidbit have contacted representatives of Rihanna for comment.

    Rihanna and A$AP’s Baby girl is a ‘Leo’

    If the rumors are true, the baby girl, whose name is not yet known is set to be a Leo, born at the start of August.

    Leo’s are known to be ‘the stars of the zodiac’ and ‘natural born leaders.’ As per In Style, Leo’s are:

    “Typical sun in Leo traits include being confident, comfortable being the center of attention, drama-adoring, ambitious, loyal, fiercely protective of their nearest and dearest, generous, luxury-loving, sunny, and big-hearted.”

    Rihanna herself is a Pisces, while dad A$AP is a Libra. Their first child, RZA was born on May 13, making him a Taurus.

    Fans are over the moon

    With the reported news taking over Twitter, fans are rushing to social media to share their excitement and congratulate the couple.

    “Leos we up!!! Congratulations to the queen” wrote one.

    “A boy and a girl, wow, congratulations to her! Motherhood is divine,” penned another.

    One exclaimed: “Rihanna gave birth to a Leo girl! I LOVE Leo girls.”

    “yasss now give us a 3rd child, AN ALBUM!” joked another.
