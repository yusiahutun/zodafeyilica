    One of Amazon’s best-kept secrets is its luxury fashion. For as long as I’ve had Prime, I’ve exclusively used it for under-$30 wardrobe staples, not realizing that there was an entire lineup of celebrity-favorite brands ready for me to shop. (Being able to add a Coach bag and toilet paper into one cart was the hack my credit card might wish I hadn’t learned.) Ahead of Amazon’s Prime Big Deal Days, I’m giving myself the go-ahead to shop those high-end pieces (as well as countless other essentials) while they’re on major sale. The first brand I’m starting with? Joe’s Jeans.

    Joe’s Jeans is a brand that’s been worn by Jennifer Garner; she once told Glamour that the brand makes closet staples that fit “just right.” And right now, a number of its fall-perfect jeans are on sale at Amazon, with prices starting at just $69.

    Best Joe’s Jeans Deals:

    Callie Jeans

    The Callie Jeans are the brand’s best deal at Amazon right now for 65 percent off. The high-rise, bootcut style is fitted through the waist, hips, and legs and flares at the calf, with fans of the jeans across the internet calling the silhouette “very flattering.” These jeans are a must-have for anyone looking to deviate from their tried-and-true skinnies but who aren’t quite ready to hop on this year’s baggy jeans trend. Plus, the look has been worn by celebrities like Jennifer Aniston and Jennifer Lopez (when all the Jens wear it, you know it’s good).

    Icon Ankle Jeans

    But if you’re solely loyal to skinny jeans (if it ain't broke, don’t fix it!) consider the brand’s Icon Ankle Jeans, which start at just $87 in select sizes. This style is made from a cotton and spandex denim blend, giving the jeans a little stretch and a lot of softness. According to shoppers, these are the “comfiest jeans;” one person wrote that the little bit of stretch allows them to adjust to their actual size and called the fit “perfect.” And per another fan, these jeans have a “great fit,” are “comfortable,” and not “as tight as former years’ skinny” jeans.

    Joe’s Jeans is a celebrity-favorite brand that rarely goes on sale, so I’m making sure to stock up while prices start at just $69 before Prime Big Deal Days. Shop more on-sale styles below.
