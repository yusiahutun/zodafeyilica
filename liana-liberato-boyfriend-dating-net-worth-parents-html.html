    The Love Relationship Between Liana Liberto & Her Boyfriend: Relationship Goals?

    Liana Liberato has been dating Instagram star Thomas O'Brien or as people know him, Tommy, since 2015. Her boyfriend has appeared the primary male protagonist on Justin Bieber's "Let Me Love You," video and is followed by more than 19k followers on Instagram.

    Well, speaking of Instagram, Liana and Tommy has been very vocal about their relationship on this social site, and they have done it so much that the fans often call them relationship goals, as the posts too adorable to be true.

    Moreover, Liana's Valentine's Day post shows how much the two are loved up.

    Lian's boyfriend, Tommy too took over Instagram to share his own Valentine's Day post, where they were spotted spending the day of love at a tree house.

    Furthermore, Tommy wishing his love mate on their anniversary beautiful in words and picture shows the value he has for her.

    The couple is not shy of making public appearances together, and an example of that was reportedly seen when they attended last year's Halloween party all loved up.

    All we can say is that the couple is adorable and we do hope that the love just keeps on growing strong between these love birds.

    A Short Wiki-Like Bio of Liana Liberato: 

    Born on August 20, 1995, in Galveston, Texas, United States, to parents George Liberato and Rhondelle Liberato, Liana Liberato is an American actress who is best known for her roles in "The Best of Me" alongside Luke Bracey, "Trust", "The Last Sin Eater" and "Stuck In Love" alongside Nat Wolff.

    Her first break came in  "Galveston: The Musical" when she was aged only 7, and since then she has honed her talents and skills to greater effect and has been a successful actress. Not only is she successful in her career, but she is also successful in her finances by being involved in some hit projects. But no official statement regarding the actual figure of her net worth has been provided by the actress.

    Regardless of that, it can be safe to say that she is one to watch out for, in the future!
