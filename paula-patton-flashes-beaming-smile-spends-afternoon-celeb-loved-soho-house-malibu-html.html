    Paula Patton flashes beaming smile as she spends an afternoon at celeb-loved Soho House in Malibu

    26 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Paula Patton is enjoying some rare downtime as she juggles the demands of a successful acting career and the duties that come with being a single mom.

    On Thursday, the Déjà Vu star swung by the celeb-loved Soho House in Malibu, otherwise known as Little Beach House Malibu.

    The members-only social club is described 'as a place for creative thinkers to meet, socialize, eat, drink and relax.'

    Patton flashed a beaming smile in the direction of passersby upon her afternoon arrival.

    She made her way through the parking lot, not far from the entrance, in a pair of faded high-waisted jeans that highlighted her toned legs.

    Patton layered a stylish mustard-yellow jacket over a low-cut white tank top to complete the look.

    In keeping with her easygoing aura, she also donned a pair of black and yellow hightop sneakers.

    The Mission: Impossible – Ghost Protocol actress had her lightened locks styled straight and she sported a face of natural makeup.

    She eagerly made her way inside to socialize and enjoy food and drinks as well as the incredible view of the Pacific Ocean.

    It appears Patton tries to incorporate spiritual guidance into her life on a regular basis, which she shared in an Instagram post on Wednesday.

    While sitting in her car, she was reading her 'favorite blue book', The Quiet Mind by White Eagle (1983), and wanted to share a few lines that inspire her when 'life can be crazy.'

    She reads the book everyday by randomly flipping to a page that 'the Universe or God guide me too.'

    But over the last couple of weeks she has found herself transfixed on one specific page and decided to post it for her 467,000 followers.

    'It's short but I love it,' she begins, before stating the headline, 'The Only Reality.'

    She continues, 'Now be happy, be filled with joy and look forward into the light. Live and move in that golden eternal light, and know that nothing can hurt you. The only reality is the light, is God, is love.'

    After taking a moment for the words to sink in, the leading lady flashed a sweet smile and said, 'I thought I'd share that with you today. Know that we're always being protected. Have a wonderful day, bye.'

    When sharing the words of wisdom on Instagram, she posted a photo of herself holding up the book.

    The Quiet Mind has been described on Google Books as 'a collection of sayings based on the theme of following the master within. White Eagle's gentle voice seeks to help us to hear the voice of our own heart. Each saying gives guidance on bringing peace and harmony into one's life.'

    As for her career, Patton last starred in The Devil's Promise, which is the latest TV series from MBC Studios that began streaming on Shahid VIP last September.

    Directed by Colin Teague, the drama-thriller focuses on 'a happily married Egyptian man and an American widow' who are 'both cheated by the devil, with devastating consequences, and they join forces and set out to beat Iblis at his own game,' according to IMDb.

    Along with Patton, the series stars Amr Youssef, Ahmed Magdy and Fathi Abdulwahhab in the lead roles with Aïcha Ben Ahmed, Yagoub Alfarhan, Mariam Alkhosht, Mourad Makram, Amr Gamal and Amr Gamal.

    Patton has been single since her divorce from singer Robin Thicke, which was finalized in March 2015, after 21 years together and almost nine years of marriage.

    After a messy separation, where she accused the singer of infidelity, domestic violence and drug and alcohol addiction, the former couple reached a custody agreement regarding their son Julian, 12, in August 2017.
