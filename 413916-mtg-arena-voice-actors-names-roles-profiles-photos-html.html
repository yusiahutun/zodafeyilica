    1. Jace Beleren – Nolan North

    Nolan Ramsey North is an American voice actor popular for providing David's English voice and motion capture performance in The Last of Us. He is the current Jace voice actor in MTG Arena.

    Nolan was born on October 31, 1970, in New Haven, Connecticut, United States of America. He has been married to Jill Murray since June 4, 1999, and they have two children.

    The talented voice artist has come out as a leader in the video game industry, considering the numerous high-profile gigs he has done. North voiced Nathan Drake in the Uncharted series, Desmond Miles in the Assassin’s Creed series, and Penguin in Batman: Arkham City and Batman: Arkham Origins.
