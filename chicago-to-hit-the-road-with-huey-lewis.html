    Chicago To Hit The Road With Huey Lewis

    (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Classic pop stars Chicago and Huey Lewis and the News plan to team up this summer for a national tour, hitting more than 30 cities across the country. The tour will kick off June 9th in Concord, California, and wrap up on July 29th in New Jersey.

    Chicago is touring in support of the group’s 30th release, titled “Chicago XXX.” This year will mark the 40th consecutive year the band has hit the road.

    Huey Lewis and the News are gearing up for the release of a “Greatest Hits” album on May 23rd. The band has amassed 17 Gold and Platinum certificates, and sales ofm ore than 12 million albums.

    The complete list of Chicago/Huey Lewis and the News dates are as follows:
