    Ariel Winter’s Blonde Hair — Another Quick Change

    Months after changing from a brunette to a red head, Ariel was ecstatic to show her fans that she dyed her hair again. As soon as she changed her hair color to blonde, she uploaded a picture to show off her followers on Instagram on May 24. She said, “Back to blonde #fridaychange #blondie #imnaturallyblonde #nicetobeback #whatdoyouthink #selfie #blondeselfie #weekend #hairchange #hair.”

    We are so used to seeing Ariel with dark hair — but we love the change!

    Back To Blonde — Other Celebs With Natural Blonde Hair

    Ariel isn’t the only celeb in Hollywood who has recently gone back to blonde locks! Celebrities such as Emma Stone and Ariel’s Modern Family co-star Sofia Vergara shocked fans when they decided to go back to their natural blonde colors earlier this year. They look great! What a perfect shade to go back to during the summer!
