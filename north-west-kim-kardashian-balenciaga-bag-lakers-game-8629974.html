    North West and Kim Kardashian just proved once again that they are total fashion twins.

    The mother-daughter duo stepped out to watch the Los Angeles Lakers take on the Golden State Warriors at the Crypto.com Arena in LA. Naturally, the pair wore their finest black leather Balenciaga for the occasion.

    Kardashian went for a sleek, chic, long black leather trench paired with black panta-leggings and a silver mirror hourglass bag — all Balenciaga, of course. After all, Kim is a Balenciaga ambassador. Last month, she wore the brand's black lace maxi dress and kept the tag dangling down her back — you know, because she just loves repping Balenciaga so much.

    As for West, she opted for a more youthful ensemble, with a bulky black leather jacket, baggy black jeans, and black sneakers, topping things off with a pair of fun space buns. However, like her mom, she also repped the Kardiashian-favorite brand, toting Balenciaga's large mesh bag.

    While the bag may look, at a glance, like a casual tote perfect for game day, it is, in fact, encrusted with rhinestones, putting the price tag at a staggering $10,990.

    This is just the latest addition to West's designer bag collection. In fact, back in 2021, the then-8-year-old took to TikTok to show off a closet filled with thousand-dollar bags by the likes of Judith Leiber, Christian Dior, Fendi, and Louis Vuitton.

    West and Kardashian clearly love sitting courtside together. West posted a cute series of selfies from the day on TikTok, which showed the pair enjoying the game and even posing with Will Ferrell.
