    Best Dresses

    Johanna Ortiz Pradera Geométrica Fringed Linen Midi Dress

    Best Dresses

    Johanna Ortiz Pradera Geométrica Fringed Linen Midi Dress

    Credit: Moda Operandi

    Colombia-based label Johanna Ortiz is a must for light, breezy dresses and pareos to wear on the beach or for tropical weddings. Expect playful patterns with pops of color.

    Sizes: 0–8

    Materials: Linen

    Best for Nights Out

    Louisa Ballou Yellow Graphic Cardigan

    Best for Nights Out

    Louisa Ballou Yellow Graphic Cardigan

    Credit: Ssense

    If you plan to let loose on your getaway, you’ll want something from Louisa Ballou, a brand that specializes in form-fitting dresses and tops complete with sheer details and deep cutouts.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Materials: 95% cupro, 5% elastane

    Best One-Stop Shop

    Cult Gaia Floreana Dress

    Best One-Stop Shop

    Cult Gaia Floreana Dress

    Credit: Cult Gaia

    From nature-inspired bags to sculptural shoes and woven dresses, Cult Gaia can help you pull together your glamorous vacation wardrobe from head to toe.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Materials: 100% cotton

    Best Accessories

    Hereu Galdana Sandal

    Best Accessories

    Hereu Galdana Sandal

    Credit: HereU

    Made in Spain, Hereu’s elegant shoes and bags are a must for both minimalists and those drawn to handcrafted pieces. You can wear these comfortable flat sandals to walk miles down the beach.

    Sizes: 35–41 EU

    Materials: Leather

    Best Classic Resortwear

    Pucci Swirling Satin Maxi Dress

    Best Classic Resortwear

    Pucci Swirling Satin Maxi Dress

    Credit: Saks

    Embrace bright palettes and psychedelic prints for your next trip. Pucci’s graphic patterns can be spotted from a mile away.

    Sizes: 4–12

    Materials: 95% Viscose, 5% elastane

    Best Casual

    Farm Rio Palermo Playsuit

    Best Casual

    Farm Rio Palermo Playsuit

    Credit: Net-a-Porter

    Brazilian label Farm Rio offers playful, happy prints in lightweight fabrics like cotton and linen. Break out a piece from this brand as you sit poolside with a caipirinha in hand.

    Sizes: XXS–XL

    Materials: 55% linen, 45% viscose

    Best for Beach Photo Shoots

    Zimmermann Alight Printed Scarf-Tie Bikini

    Best for Beach Photo Shoots

    Zimmermann Alight Printed Scarf-Tie Bikini

    Credit: Saks

    Zimmermann is a favorite of influencers and social media aficionados, thanks to its mini hemlines, ruffle details, and colorful prints. The Australian label is begging to star in your next vacation photo shoot.

    Sizes: 0–3

    Materials: 80% polyamide, 20% elastane

    Best for Italy

    La Double J Bold Shoulder Minidress

    Best for Italy

    La Double J Bold Shoulder Minidress

    Credit: Shopbop

    Its designs inspired by founder J.J. Martin’s collection of vintage clothing, La Double J is a bold ready-to-wear and home brand meant for the streets of Portofino.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Materials: 98% cotton, 2% elastane

    Best Jewelry

    Eliou Kruze Necklace

    Best Jewelry

    Eliou Kruze Necklace

    Credit: Eliou

    Miami-based Eliou is a jewelry label with a playful disposition and a knack for mixing and matching colors and textures. Add a bold necklace or bracelet to your night-out looks.

    Materials: Resin, quartz, ceramic, Indian agate, enamel, and gold-filled beads

    Best Caftans

    Emporio Sirenuse Giada Kaftan

    Best Caftans

    Emporio Sirenuse Giada Kaftan

    Credit: Net-a-Porter

    For throw-on-and-go cover-ups when you’re heading from your room to the pool, try a loose caftan. Emporio Sirenuse draws inspiration from the Italian coast, with lightweight cover-ups imbued with elegant details.

    Sizes: 36–50 IT

    Materials: Cotton

    Best Luxury Swimwear

    Eres Dancing One-Piece

    Best Luxury Swimwear

    Eres Dancing One-Piece

    Credit: Eres

    Simple, sleek, and luxurious, Eres is known for its high-quality swimwear and cover-ups. You’d fit right in lounging at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc.

    Sizes: 38–44

    Materials: 84% polyamide, 16% spandex

    Best for Being Out and About

    Alemais Porcelain Printed Silk-Satin Shirt

    Best for Being Out and About

    Alemais Porcelain Printed Silk-Satin Shirt

    Credit: Moda Operandi

    Australian label Alemais’s on-trend designs are perfect for packing on a vacation where you’ll be doing some urban exploring, visiting wine bars or local cafés.

    Sizes: 0–12

    Materials: Silk

    Best Crochet

    Nia Thomas Celina Swimsuit

    Best Crochet

    Nia Thomas Celina Swimsuit

    Credit: Nia Thomas

    When you’re somewhere warm, clothes need to be breathable, and Nia Thomas’s cool crochet fits the bill.

    Sizes: XS–2XL

    Materials: 100% Tanguis cotton

    Best Dressy

    Agua by Agua Bendita Vivianne Dress

    Best Dressy

    Agua by Agua Bendita Vivianne Dress

    Credit: Net-a-Porter

    This Colombian label’s elegant dresses embrace the breeziness of resortwear, but with an elevated twist. Break out the brand for formal events on your tropical getaway.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Materials: 100% cotton

    Best Sandals

    Ancient Greek Sandals Satira Sandals

    Best Sandals

    Ancient Greek Sandals Satira Sandals

    Credit: Ancient Greek Sandals

    Parade up and down the streets of Spalato or St. Barts in a pair of comfortable flat sandals that can be easily stowed in your suitcase. This timeless brand has long been a go-to for summer footwear.

    Sizes: 35–42 EU

    Materials: Leather
