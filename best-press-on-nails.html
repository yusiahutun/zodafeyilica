    Meet the Experts

    • Gina Edwards is a celebrity nail artist and Kiss brand ambassador based in New York City.

    What are the longest-lasting press-on nails?

    Depending on your lifestyle, press-on nails can typically last anywhere from a week to two weeks. Adding a few drops of glue to your natural nail and the extension, and gently squeezing it in place for at least 30 seconds can help ensure maximum hold.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    How do you make press-on nails look real?

    If you're in search of a work-friendly set of press-ons to finish off your beauty look without going overboard, opt for shorter round or square nails that won't get in the way of typing an email. And since these staples are easily manipulated, you can also file press-ons according to your preference for a more natural look.
