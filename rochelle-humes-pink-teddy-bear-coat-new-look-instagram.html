    Rochelle Humes is giving us all the autumnal vibes right now with her high street wardrobe. On Wednesday, the mother-of-two stepped out in a fabulous teddy bear jacket - which was made in the prettiest of pastel pinks. The cosy winter coat looked high end, but we were shocked to discover it only set her back just £29.99 from New Look! The fabulous outerwear staple comes in eleven different colours and is currently available online in all sizes. The 29-year-old teamed her statement coat with a simple pair of black trousers and crisp white trainers as she posed up a storm in her latest snap on her Instagram page. She wrote: "Out and about filming and it’s officially AUTUMN not FALL." Referencing her snuggly teddy coat, she added: "Also, if you want a bear hug, I'm your girl!"

    Rochelle cosied up in her New Look coat

    The most famous teddy bear coat has to be the iconic MaxMara version which has been worn by everyone from Kim Kardashian to Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and they are priced at just under £2,000 a pop. Thank goodness for New Look coming to our rescue with a purse-friendly version.

    £29.99, New Look

    Rochelle has a collection with the popular high street store, which was announced in February. Since then, the former Saturdays singer has released a selection of edits for each season and they have proved super popular amongst shoppers. The TV presenter announced the exciting news on her Instagram feed earlier in the year, whilst she was shooting the glam, accompanying campaign.

    READ: We think everyone will be wearing Rochelle Humes' £27 stripy jumper this winter

    Featuring a glamorous shot of her sitting upon a sun-drenched chair with the New Look branding printed over the top, she wrote: "BEYOND excited to announce that I am now the face of New Look. If you'd have told this to my teenage self I would have never believed you. Having the best time shooting our first campaign in Marrakech, stay tuned for all the exciting things to come #RochelleForNewlook #PINCHME"
