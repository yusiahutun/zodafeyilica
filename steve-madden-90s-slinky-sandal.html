    We've seen the return of the choker, denim overalls, silky slip dresses and more, but now the ultimate '90s shoe is officially making a comeback too.

    You know the shoe. If you listen close enough, you can still hear the distinct clopping sound of it walking by. It was the sandal that you begged your parents to buy you at the mall, the sandal that every cool girl had a pair of, the sandal that you justified wearing with literally any and every outfit you owned: the Steve Madden Slinky.

    Steve Madden

    Continuing in the ongoing/never-ending revival of '90s style, Steve Madden announced it's re-releasing the iconic platform sandal for Summer 2017. Twenty years later and the shoe still features its signature flatform sole and stretchy upper, practically begging to be paired with a denim skirt and baby tee once again.

    Since we've yet to discover how to time-travel out of 2017 and back to 1995, bringing back the most nostalgic shoe will have to do as the next best thing, it seems.

    You can pre-order the Steve Madden Slinky sandals for $70 now at stevemadden.com.
