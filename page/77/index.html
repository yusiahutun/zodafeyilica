Phish Announce The 4-Day Mondegreen Festival In Dover For 2024

DOVER, Deleware (CelebrityAccess) — The legendary jam band Phish is set to host Mondegreen, a four-day festival set for this August 15-18 at The Woodlands in Dover, DE. The festival, which is the 11th the band has hosted, will see Phish perform on each of the event’s four nights, alongside a curated lineup of fax experience, culinary offerings, and art exhibitions.
