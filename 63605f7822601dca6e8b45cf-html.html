    The notoriety that Saúl "Canelo" Álvarez has achieved has been a reason for admiration by many and criticism from others, but the fame of the undisputed super middleweight champion reached the third baseman of the Los Angeles Dodgers, Justin Turner, who took advantage of his resemblance to the Mexican boxer to dress up at a Halloween party.

    Canelo and Turner, the two favorite redheads in sports?

    Costume parties are a constant during these dates, where thousands of people bring out their best outfits to be fashionable, but the Dodgers player did not hesitate to characterize himself as Canelo Alvarez.

    The baseball player posted some pictures on his social networks in which, thanks to his beard and reddish hair, it was easier to look like the red-haired Mexican who is considered one of the best boxers today.

    With a boxer's outfit, as if he was already going to fight, Justin Turner's was just like the one the Mexican champion wears, even with the name of the sponsors. The red cap and gloves used by the boxer had the initials of Canelo and also the name of the brand No Boxing No Life.

    Justin Turner accepts Jake Paul's calls

    But that was not all, because in another photo he is seen wearing a robe, like the one boxers wear before getting to the ring, and if his wife was characterized with the pugilism theme, as one of the women who announces in the ring each round.

    "Can you believe that Jake Paul called me?", wrote in his social networks the baseball player, who surely admires Canelo, who last week had surgery on his left hand and it is unknown how many months it will take to return to activity, and although it is expected to be in May, it could be until September, everything will depend on the evolution of his hand.
