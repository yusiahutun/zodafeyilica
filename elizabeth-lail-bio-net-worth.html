    The Actress Elizabeth Dean Lail is famous for portraying the role of Anna in the ABC adventure fantasy series Once Upon a Time. She has managed to win the hearts of millions of fans all across the world for the roles she has portrayed from 2011 to the age of 24.

    When was Elizabeth Lail born?

    Elizabeth Lail entered this earth as Elizabeth Dean Lail on the 25th of March, 1992 in Williamson County, Texas, USA. As of 2019, her age is 27 years old under the birth sign Aries. She was born to parents Dean Franklin Lail (father) and Kay Lurene Surratt (mother). She grew up along with her older sister Kathryn Dean Lail in Asheboro, North Carolina.

    Lail holds an American nationality and belongs to a white ethnical background. At a young age, she developed a passion for acting. Elizabeth stands at a height of 5 feet 8 inches with an average weight. She has brown light hair color and green color eyes.

    Relationship with her Boyfriend Julian

    No, Elizabeth Lail is not a married woman. She has been in a relationship with an Australian model named Julian Haig since 2015. Interestingly, she has not spoken about her relationship with Julian since 2016. Her relationship has never been included in any problems and it seems that the couple will get married sometime soon.

    Elizabeth loves traveling and has been to many popular destinations across the world. Also, she often posts pictures clicked at various holiday destinations on her Instagram page. Likewise, she loves hanging around with her friends.

    Besides, she is an animal lover and raises many pet dogs among other animals. Currently, she lives in New York City, New York where she often travels to Los Angeles, California as part of her work.

    Education and Early Career

    Elizabeth Lail attended ‘Asheboro High School’ from where she graduated in 2010. Then she went to the ‘University of North Carolina School of the Arts where she studied acting. Before graduating with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in May 2014, she was part of students’ film productions, such as ‘Model Airplane’.

    After graduating from university, she went to New York City to pursue a career in theatre. Then she started working as a babysitter during the day to support herself. Though she initially wanted to establish a career in theatre, she changed her mind after auditioning for a role in ‘Once Upon a Time.

    Career

    • After six months of graduating from college, Elizabeth Lail started her career in the role of Anna in the fourth season of ‘Once Upon a Time.’ In the 10 episodes of the series, she played the role of Princess Anna.
    • There Her performance increased popularity who became a household name in the United States.
    • In the year 2016, she appeared in the popular supernatural horror television series Dead of Summer’ as Amy Hughes.
    • In the 10 episodes of the series, she played the role of camp counselor Amy. It was aired on the ‘Freeform’ TV channel from June 28 to August 30, 2016.
    • The next year, she appeared in one of the episodes of the long-running crime thriller television series ‘The Blacklist’ as Natalie Luc.
    • Besides, she also appeared in the female lead role of Guinevere Beck in a psychological thriller television series titled ‘You’. It premiered in the United States on September 9, 2018, on the ‘Lifetime’ television channel.
    • In 10 episodes of the series, she played Guinevere Beck along with stars Penn Badgley and Shay Mitchell.
    • The same year, she played Lea in the 2018 Anja Murmann-directed drama-thriller film ‘Unintended’. Also, she was seen playing Emily Chapin in one of the episodes of ‘CBS’ network’s web television series ‘The Good Fight’.
    • In the year 2019, Elizabeth appeared as Quinn in a thriller film titled ‘Countdown’.
    • Apart from her acting career, she also focuses on charity works. She takes part in a fundraising program for a charity organization named ‘Space on Ryder Farm’ which aims at creating a safe environment to help budding artists express themselves.
    • In the year 2019, she supported the American Red Cross’s initiative of installing 100,000 free smoke alarms in more than 100 communities that are at risk of facing fire accidents.
    • She is active on social media accounts where she raises awareness of several issues.
