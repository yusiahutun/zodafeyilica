    With all the hype around Travis and Taylor during the Super Bowl, other celebrity attendees, including Leonardo DiCaprio may have skipped the limelight, but luckily, the celeb cam was on hand.

    Sunday night saw Travis Kelce and The Kansas City Chiefs take home the all-important Super Bowl title, and a number of A-list celebrities were in the crowd to witness the historic moment. Most, rightfully so, looked to be having their life on the Jumbotron, but the same couldn’t be said about Leonardo DiCaprio.

    Leonardo DiCaprio’s Super Bowl ‘celeb cam’ is nothing short of disappointing

    One of the best parts of any big sporting event is seeing which celebs are ‘called out’ by the celeb cam, and Super Bowl LVIII did not disappoint, but Leo’s reaction did.

    Jeff Goldblum got the crowd going with a wholesome hand-heart and some killer dance moves, while Hailey and Justin Bieber treated it like ‘kiss cam’.

    However, the Titanic star had somewhat of a disappointing reaction, as he barely seemed to look up from his phone. When he did eventually look up, it wasn’t for long, and he got back to what must have been an important text.

    Of course, fans took to TikTok to joke about what the star was up to, as one penned: “Leo is in the middle of breaking up with his 24-year-old girlfriend.”

    “Leo look up you’re on camera,” said another.

    The 49-year-old has been linked to 25-year-old Italian model, Vittoria Ceretti, since the end of last year. Last month, they were seen jetting off on vacation with his family, and BFF Tobey Maguire.

    The star’s dating life is ‘poked fun’ of by Nickelodeon

    Everyone who has a social media account has seen the running joke of Leo ‘dumping’ girlfriends after they ‘pass the age of 25’.

    Now, it looks like the jokes have extended beyond the internet, and made their way to children’s TV channel Nickelodeon, which had some iconic coverage of the big game, with commentators including SpongeBob SquarePants.

    The camera panned to the star when the ball was placed on the 25-yard line. “Leonardo DiCaprio, 25 – that’s his dating history,” SpongeBob said before bursting into laughter.

    Who knew the sponge had it in him?
