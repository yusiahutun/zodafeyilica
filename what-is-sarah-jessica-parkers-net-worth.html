    Sarah Jessica Parker’s net worth reflects the actress’s successful career. Once one of the highest-paid actors, here’s how much she’s worth.

    Sarah Jessica Parker was born in Nelsonville, Ohio, on the 25th of March, 1965. She’s the fourth child among eight siblings. Her family moved to New York so she could get specialized training in acting. Sarah had always been passionate about the art world from a young age. She attended New York’s professional children’s school of creative and performing arts and Hollywood’s high school in Los Angeles.

    In 1976 when she was 11 years, Sarah secured herself a role in the Broadway revival of William Archibald’s play “The innocents.” Her performance in the movie led to countless other roles in films like “Annie,” “Square Pegs,” “Footloose,” and  “Girls Just Want To Have Fun.”

    She has also starred in “Flight Navigator,” “L.A. Story,” “Honeymoon in Vegas,” “Hocus Pocus,” “Miami Rhapsody,” “Sex and The City,” and many others. Throughout her career, Sarah has received Six Globe Awards, two Primetime Emmy Awards, an American Comedy Award, and a People’s Choice Award, as well as numerous accolades for her impeccable talent and contribution to the art world.

    Besides acting, Sarah Jessica is also a Model, Fashion designer, TV host, Film producer, Singer, and brand owner. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the talented star has made about $200 million from all her business and career endeavors. 

    Sarah Wore a Black Dress to Her Wedding

    Sarah might’ve stunned fans in a big white wedding gown in the movie “Sex and the City,” but when she walked down the aisle with Matthew Broderick in 1997, the actress amazed the world by showing up in an iconic black Morgan Le Fay dress to her low-key wedding. 

    However, more than 19 years later, Sarah revealed that she should have made a better decision. Although many consider the dress a significant fashion choice, the actress revealed she felt too embarrassed to marry in white. Sarah, however, confessed that she regrets showing up in the black gown. The actress wished to return in time and appear in a proper beautiful white wedding dress.

    Sarah Jessica Wanted To Be a Ballerina

    Sarah Jessica attended New York’s School of American Ballet, where she was trained in the practice from a little girl. She, however, had to give up her dreams of dancing after breaking into the film industry in the 80s. In an interview with Hello! Magazine, Sarah admitted that ballerina was the one hobby she truly enjoyed more than acting.

    Even though she still dreams of performing, she insists that she will never force her twin daughters, Marion and Tabitha, to chase after her dreams.

    Sarah Jessica Has Been Married for More Than 25 Years

    Sarah got married to actor Matthew Broderick in 1997. Their desire to work in the theater, being surrounded by people whose work they were in awe of, and simply becoming good actors, not thinking of fame or money, brought the two together.
