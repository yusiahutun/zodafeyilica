    Growing up, I used to hate wearing dresses. I was always a bit of a tomboy in that way. They were always itchy, difficult to get on and off, and I couldn’t just flip around like I wanted to. Now that I’m older, I have a bit more tolerance for fussy dress styles, but if I have the choice, I’m reaching for a T-shirt dress. 

    They’re comfortable, easily pop over my head, and can be dressed up or down depending on your shoes and accessories. But what if there was a style that had all the makings of a classic T-shirt dress, but was elevated enough that you didn’t need accessories to dress it up? Well, that versatile style would be the Anrabess Tie-Waist T-Shirt Midi Dress on Amazon.

    Anrabess Tie-Waist T-Shirt Midi Dress

    The midi dress comes in 19 colors, including black, brown, navy, green, pink, and purple. Sizes range from S through XXL, and it has a close-to-body fit with lots of stretch, and a tie detail at the waist to accentuate the figure. It has a spacious crew neck opening so you can easily pop it on over the head, two side slits that stop at the knee so movement is restricted, and a hemline that falls around the mid-calf depending on height.

    While I don’t need much convincing on why I should add this Anrabess midi dress to my cart, Amazon shoppers raving five-star reviews certainly sealed the deal and I’ve proceeded on to check out. One shopper described the dress as “very flattering” and “comfortable” and mentioned that it “sits well around the neckline,” is form fitting “without clinging,” and is versatile enough to be worn for “dressy or casual events.” Another reviewer raved that they wanted the dress in every color, and said it “fit true to size” and was “good quality.”

    One shopper said the fabric was “stretchy,” “lightweight,” and that the tie “makes this dress fit perfectly.” A final reviewer wrote that the “T-shirt material is very comfortable,” the dark colors were great at giving a smooth appearance, and that it wasn’t sheer.
