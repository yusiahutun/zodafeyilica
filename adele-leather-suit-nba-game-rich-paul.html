    31 May 2024, 00:00

    Adele's courtside style is worthy of a double take.

    The "Easy on Me" singer made an appearance at last night's Los Angeles Lakers vs. Golden State Warriors NBA game in L.A. along with her new boyfriend, sports agent Rich Paul. For the night out, Adele opted for a sleek brown leather blazer and matching pants from Altuzarra, worn with a coordinating monogram wool coat and pointed-toe pumps by Louis Vuitton, along with a chic mini handbag also from the French fashion house. Paul was dressed casually for the pair's date night, sporting a black turtleneck, an olive-green zip-up sweater, and gray trousers.

    Adam Pantozzi//Getty Images

    Adele and Paul went Instagram official back in September when the singer shared a sweet photo of the couple at a wedding they attended together. The singer snuggled up to her boyfriend while wearing a stunning black Schiaparelli gown with a cloudlike, off-the-shoulder overlay and large gold-and-pearl teeth earrings.

    The singer has remained busy in recent weeks, working to promote her upcoming studio album, 30, which releases next month. According to Adele, the album narrates her healing process in the aftermath of her divorce from ex-husband Simon Konecki.

    "[The album is] that friend who, no matter what, checked in on me even though I'd stopped checking in with them because I'd become so consumed by my own grief," the singer wrote in a note to fans announcing the album's release. "I've painstakingly rebuilt my house and my heart since then and this album narrates it. Home is where the heart is x."
