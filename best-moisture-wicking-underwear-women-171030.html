    What is moisture-wicking underwear? 

    Most of us have heard of moisture-wicking bras, which are designed with special fabric to keep you dry during workouts. Similarly, moisture-wicking panties help to combat that moist, sticky feeling that can arise below the belt. These special panties are made of breathable materials, such as cotton and bamboo, that work to prevent sweat from getting trapped while simultaneously repelling any moisture that might occur. 

    That’s good news, since damp underwear is a breeding ground for bacteria, which can eventually lead to yeast infections. 

    An integral part of not only staying comfortable, but healthy, every woman should have a few pairs of these in her collection. When combined with body sprays, cooling bedsheets, and dehumidifiers, you’ll be well-equipped to stay nice and dry all year long from head to toe.
