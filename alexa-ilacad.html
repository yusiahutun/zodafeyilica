    Alexa Ilacad (born 26 February 2000, as Alexandra Madarang Ilacad) is a Filipina actress, singer and dancer. She appeared in a various commercials and shows of ABS-CBN. She rose to prominence when she joined the kiddie gag show Goin' Bulilit. She was first seen in TV commercials, such as Vaseline shampoo between 2004 and 2005. She was cast as Lexie Domingo in the youth-oriented comedy TV sitcom Luv U. She was paired with Nash Aguas who played the puppy love of Lexie, Benjamin Jalbuena. She and Nash Aguas was paired again to do Camille and Drew in the adaptation of a wattpad story Bagito.They had their contract signing with star cinema and they were chosen to play another Wattpad story written by iDangs Teen Clash. Aguas and Ilacad were paired again in Prime Time series The Good Son alongside with Jerome Ponce, Loisa Andalio, Mccoy De Leon, Elisse Joson and Joshua Garcia. She recently released an album under Star Records entitled To The Moon And Back. It has a total of 6 song the acoustic version of her song Kahit Saan, Kahit Kailan. She was help by Gracenote's especially it's vocalist, Eunice Jorge for her album.
