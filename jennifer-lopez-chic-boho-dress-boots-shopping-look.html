    Jennifer Lopez is ready to trade in her summer stilettos for platform boots.

    Photographed out and about with her daughter, Emme Muniz, while visiting the Immersive Van Gogh Exhibit in Hollywood, the "On the Floor" singer wore a bohemian-style white minidress paired with a cinched belt and seriously tall tan leather knee-high boots. Lopez accessorized the look with a matching brown leather Diana Handbag courtesy of Gucci, her signature hoop earrings, layered necklaces, and aviator-style sunglasses. The standout boots seem to be in regular rotation for Lopez, as she previously wore the same pair with a similar shirtdress last year while on a date with her then fianc√©, Alex Rodriguez.

    BACKGRID

    Lately, the singer has been busy focusing on her rekindled relationship with actor and director Ben Affleck, whom she was previously engaged to in 2002. After a few weeks of being photographed together on low-key outings and dinner dates, the pair finally went Instagram official last month to mark Lopez's 52nd birthday.

    According to People, though the power couple have been dating again for only a few months, they're both focused on gradually blending their families and the future they can build together.

    "This is not a casual relationship," a source told the outlet. "They are taking it seriously and want it to be long-lasting." The insider added, "They will continue to travel back and forth between Los Angeles and Miami. They are very happy together."

    "They are just genuinely enjoying each other's company and having a great time," another source told Entertainment Tonight. "Jen is very supportive of Ben and his career, but she is also connected, successful and has her own life."
