    A well-written wedding invitation message gives a person an idea of what to expect on your big day. The diction gives each guest a clue about the overall tone. If you are using hard-copy or e-cards, guests should get to know details like the colour scheme.

    Best wedding invitation message: what to write

    Weddings are joyous ceremonies that bring together the groom, bride, and their families and friends. It can be challenging to find the right words to use when inviting people to celebrate your big day.

    In the past, invitations were mainly made via hard-copy cards. Today, you can request your loved ones to attend your function via SMS, WhatsApp messages, and even other social media platforms.

    How do I invite friends to my wedding?

    You can invite friends to witness your special day through a hard-copy card, SMS, WhatsApp message, or even an e-card.

    How do you write a good wedding invitation?

    If you have wondered how to write a wedding invitation message, fret not. The message should include hosts' names, date, venue, time, and post-ceremony plans. note that the recipient matters, e.g., a sister wedding invitation message is less formal than one for a colleague.

    How do I invite people to a wedding via WhatsApp?

    If you have wondered how to invite people to your wedding via WhatsApp, you can create an e-card, plain text, or video requesting people to grace your event.

    What should I ask guest speakers during a virtual wedding?

    A virtual wedding is a nuptial ceremony where the officiant, guests, and couple are joined together by a video call. Some of the questions to ask guest speakers during a virtual event are what they wish for the next chapter of your lives and what qualities they most admire in you as a couple.
