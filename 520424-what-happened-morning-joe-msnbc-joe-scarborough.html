    What happened to Morning Joe on MSNBC?

    The show still airs every weekday from 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. ET (Eastern time). Its presenters include Scarborough, Mika Brzezinski and Willie Geist as co-hosts. The show began on 9 April 2007, replacing Imus in the Morning on MSNBC and has been on air since.

    Where is Joe Scarborough?

    He is still the co-host of Morning Joe on MSNBC and has not left the show. On 31 July 2023, Scarborough took a break to spend time with his family, recover from health issues and reportedly write a new book. Though initially reported as a two-week break, the host was back on air on 2 August 2023.

    In late August, the leading talk show host was absent again. According to the show's video timelines on YouTube, Scarborough was missing for one week, from 28 August 2023 to 3 September 2023. He came back on air on 4 September 2023.

    As a result of his frequent absence, Scarborough has prompted rumours that he is working on a new project. However, there is not enough evidence to support the claim. The other claim is that he is taking some time off due to his health.

    Joe Scarborough's illness

    The talk show host has not publicly announced his health status but had previously stated his struggles with depression and anxiety. As a result, fans have speculated that his occasional absence from television is due to health problems.

    In 2003, Scarborough suffered a heart attack that set him back. The television presenter underwent five surgeries to repair the damage caused by the attack. In November 2017, the host took time away from television to recover from a medical procedure, presumably one of the five repair surgeries.

    What happened to Mika on Morning Joe?

    Mika Brzezinski is still the co-host of Morning Joe and has not left. Mika, also the wife of fellow broadcaster, has been on the talk show since its inception in 2007. However, like her husband, Mika took a break on 31 July 2023 but returned on 2 August 2023. She took another break on 28 August 2023, returning on air on 5 September 2023.
