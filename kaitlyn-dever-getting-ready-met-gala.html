    19 Aug 2024, 00:00

    "I’m a little worried about the steps!” It is the morning of Kaitlyn Dever’s first ever Met Gala and the actress, who made her name in the cult classic Booksmart, and who held her own against Hollywood heavyweights George Clooney and Julia Roberts in last summer’s Ticket to Paradise, is sharing her thoughts for the night ahead. “As long as I get past the steps…then I hear it's all the fun inside once we can start eating!”

    Last night she was determined, thanks to her “sturdy shoes”, not tumble on those iconic steps, while wearing a show-stopping red sequin Michael Kors Collection dress that was custom-made for her. But before she headed to fashion’s most famous night out, she sat down with Bazaar (and a lot of coffee) to discuss her inaugural gala, the evolution of her style, and why her mother is her forever style icon…

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    Why does your Met Gala look feel like a good fit for you?

    “When I first saw the drawing of my dress, I honestly just fell in love. It's one of my favourite colours. I love a train – and this dress has a pretty significant one. I also love the flower being a sort of nod to Karl and Chanel. The cut-outs, the crimson colour and the fact it has this hand embroidery all over it, with beautiful sequins. It's just absolutely stunning.”

    How did you decide which brand you wanted to work with?

    “It was such an organic process, working with Michael on this. I met him a year or so ago and I have always found him to be such sweet and kind man. That’s really how I decide to work with anyone or any brand, and everyone at Michael Kors throughout this whole process has been so welcoming and kind.”

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    How was working on a look for the Met Gala different from your average red-carpet styling experience?

    “I’ve worked with the team so closely on this look, as I do with my stylist, Ryan Hastings, who I trust so much. But getting a look together for the Met has felt really different from a normal red carpet. You get such an insight into how much work goes into a dress like this and there are so many more fittings to get it to look perfect. It really shows you how fashion is art.”

    What is your ideal getting-ready set up?

    “My plan is to have a long, warm bath and hang out there for a while. More coffee probably. Then music is key. I have to put on music. Maybe some John Denver, or I've been listening to a lot of Eric Clapton recently. When the nerves kick in, I might need a quick glass of champagne too…”

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    What kind of fashion makes you feel your most confident?

    “Honestly, I love being in sweatpants because I feel my most confident when I’m comfortable. But I do like to go bold with my fashion, and when I'm doing something bold, but I'm comfy, that's when I feel most confident. And that's how I feel in this dress actually. It really flows, and my body feels comfortable. I think it shows the true evolution of my style. I’ve gone from wearing soccer shorts with everything 24/7 as a kid to loving Audrey Hepburn. That journey has for sure shaped how I dress now.”

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    Whose style have you always admired ?

    “To be honest, no one has really shaped my style more than my mother. I’ve got to say, my mom's style is my forever favourite. What I love about her and her style, is that she really wears whatever she likes. She’s really good at finding good vintage designer pieces. She's the type of person who can point out the exact collection a pair of trousers is from. She’s just such a smart and versatile dresser and her style is one I've admired the most.”

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    Kaitlyn attending the Met Gala with Kerry Washington and Vanessa Hudgens, all wearing Michael Kors

    What has been your most memorable fashion moment?

    “I think that different characters I've played over the years have influenced my style changes. My characters in Booksmart and Short Term 12 have been the standouts. I loved my patchwork jacket in Booksmart so much – though I’m lucky, I’ve really loved everything I’ve worn, especially on red carpets. But to me, the most memorable fashion moment I’ve had is when I took my little sister to a catwalk show in Paris. It was her first time going and she loves fashion, and even makes her own clothes, so to share that with her was so special.”

    courtesy Emilio Madrid

    When do you feel most beautiful?

    “Whenever I'm with my family or with my sisters, that’s when I feel happiest and the most beautiful. Although, a friend has actually got me into taking a cold shower every morning and that has me feeling good, inside and out.”

    Why does what we wear matter?

    “I think ultimately it’s a huge representation of who you are and how you approach the world.”

    courtesy Emilio Madrid
