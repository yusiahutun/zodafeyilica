    Makeup-free Shakira rocks black shorts while showing off her wakeboarding skills in Miami

    Shakira was a makeup-free beauty as she enjoyeda day out on the water near her Miami, Florida home on Tuesday. The Colombian singer, 46, showed off her impressive wakeboarding skills while being pulled around by a speedboat with friends.

    07 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Shakira was a makeup-free beauty as she enjoyed a day out on the water near her Miami, Florida home on Tuesday.

    The Colombian singer, 46, showed off her impressive wakeboarding skills while being pulled around by a speedboat with friends.

    She hopped on the boat in a pair of black shorts and a gray long-sleeve top, which she eventually traded for a bright purple rash guard. 

    Her thick, curly hair flowed down to her waist from under a black baseball cap.

    It eventually got soaking wet as she conquered the waves.

    Shakira kept her hands out and her knees bent in order to stay on the board while being dragged around the water.

    For safety, the Hips Don't Lie hitmaker donned a blue O'Neill life jacket.  

    She was joined by the two sons she shares with ex-husband Gerard Pique: Milan, nine, and Sasha, seven.

    Shakira recently admitted that she felt 'betrayed' by her former spouse of 11 years after learning he'd allegedly been unfaithful.

    They split last year and the soccer star has since gone public with his new girlfriend Clara Chia Marti, who he's reportedly engaged to.

    Speaking to People en Español, Shakira said that she didn't think she was 'going to survive' as the news of Gerard's alleged infidelity came to light while her father, William Mebarak Chadid, was in the ICU.

    A year after announcing the end of her 11-year relationship with the Spanish soccer player, 36, the superstar, 46, recalled how her dad flew 'to Barcelona to console' her as she was 'consumed with sadness because of' their separation.

    'While he was at Milan's first communion, he was gravely injured in an accident. Everything happened at once. My home was falling apart,' she told the outlet.

    She continued: 'I was finding out through the press that I had been betrayed while my dad was in the ICU.'

    The mother-of-two, who shares two sons, Milan, ten, and Sasha, eight, with Piqué, expressed feeling devastated that she couldn't turn to dad, 91, for the advice she 'needed so much.'

    Still, she hopes they serve as 'role models' to her kids as a good example of a healthy marriage.
