    Kiele Michelle Sanchez is a famous American actress who starred in the tv series, “The Glades”, a crime drama television series that aired on the A&E network for four years. she was also cast in another drama television series, “Kingdom” on DirecTV on the audience network.

    Early life

    Kiele Sanchez was born on October 13, 1977, in Chicago, Illinois, United States of America. her father is Oscar Sanchez and her mother’s name is not yet revealed. reportedly, she has three siblings whose names are not known yet. She belongs of American nationality and is of Rican, Polish, Puerto, and French descent. She studied at Glenbard North high school, in Carol Stream, Illinois, where she started her acting career by performing in a school production of an American realist novel, “The Grapes of Wrath”.

    Kiele Sanchez Career

    In the year 2000, Sanchez auditioned for “Wanna be a VJ”, a show and contest on the MTV channel in new york. She was chosen as one of the five finalists but did not succeed in the competition. However, an agent who had seen her on MTV expressed interest in appointing her. She auditioned and played the role of “Claudia Wills” in the series, “That Was Then”, an American short-lived drama series, and as “Susan Wagner” in the sitcom, “Married to the Kellys”. both, series as well as sitcoms aired on the ABC network. In 2003, she was cast in the comedy film “Stuck on you”. Later, she appeared as “Nikki Fernandez” in the third season of the hit ABC show “Lost”, a famous drama television series, but due to her excellent acting skills, she killed off six episodes because of fan responses to her character.

    Personal life

    In 2001, Kiele married Zach Helm, a film director, producer, and screenwriter. Later, the couple divorced in 2008 due to personal reasons. Subsequently, in 2010, she began dating Zach Gilford, an actor with whom she co-starred in the ABC television film “the matadors”. In November 2011, the couple got engaged and married on the 29th of December, 2012. The couple co-starred in the 2014 horror film “The purge: anarchy”. In August 2015, Kiele announced that she and Gilford were expecting their first child, a son, but later in October 2015, unfortunately, the couple announced that Sanchez had undergone a late-term miscarriage. In November 2017, the couple was happily blessed with a daughter named Zeppelin Adele Gilford.
