    Today's Dylan Dreyer in hysterical laughter in video with husband as he teases pregnancy

    11 May 2024, 00:00

    The Today meteorologist and husband Brian Fichera have three boys together

      Dylan Dreyer was left in floods of giggles while recording her podcast with husband, Brian Fichera, and his reaction was priceless.

      In the video below, the mom-of-three can be seen roaring with laughter for an unexpected reason as they attempted to tape. 

      Dylan could hardly contain herself, made worse by her hubby's surprising pregnancy quip. The Today host previously spoke about baby number four in an exclusive interview with HELLO!.

      "I think I've maxed out now," she said with a chuckle before admitting: "I wish I had started having kids sooner, because I really love everything about kids."

      Dylan - who shares Calvin, six, Oliver, three, and one-year-old Rusty with Brian - added: "I never thought I would love it as much as I do. Being a mom is the best. I love fostering their ideas, I also somehow found more patience after becoming a mom."

      The number three also holds a special place in both Dylan and Brian's hearts as she explained: "When we decided to have children, I used to always want three. I grew up with two brothers, my husband is one of three. Three just feels right for us now."

      Dylan and Brian have their hands full parenting, their boys but they wouldn't have it any other way. "After we had Oliver, I thought I'll never do that again," she added. "But something didn't feel complete for us.

      "Both of us had a twinge that we still wanted a third child. Now we have him and everything feels right and complete."

      One look at Dylan's Instagram feed and you'd be convinced she was born to be a mom, but she admits, it wasn't always on the cards for her and Brian.

      "We didn't think we wanted kids at all," she said about her and her husband of ten years. "We were living in New York City, traveling whenever we wanted to, doing whatever we wanted, and then suddenly it sort of came to us both. We were like 'let's have kids'. Both of us obviously love it."
