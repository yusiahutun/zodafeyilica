    Frequently asked questions about these hairstyles

    Despite being one of the most stylish hair designs of 2020, a lot of questions keep cropping up about these popular hairdos. To assist you to get a better understanding, below are the answers to some of the most common questions.

    What is the Edgar haircut?

    It is a type of men's cut with short hair on the sides, back, and top. The cut can be paired with all kinds of tapered fade haircuts or an undercut. Most popular versions have a mid to high skin fade with short thick hair on top and a little length in the front.

    What is the origin of the Edgar haircut?

    Edgar Martinez was a baseballer who had gained favour among his fans. As a way to show love and appreciation, one of this fans instructed a barber to shave the player's face into his head. Much as this was done out of appreciation, the style gained popularity has been adopted by many.

    What is the real name of the designer?

    The style was designed by a barber and artist named Anthony Reyes. He designed the cut as a tribute to Edgar Martinez who had become the fifth player from the Caribbean island to get inducted to the Hall of Fame.

    What is Edgar haircut real name?

    The hairstyle has attracted the interest of many stylists across the globe. As a result, various modifications to the style have also come up with each style having its Edgar haircut name as seen above.
