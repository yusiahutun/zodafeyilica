    Natural beauty: Jodie, 52, displayed the extent of her natural good looks as she bared her make-up free face on her outing to a local book shop

    After stacking up countless Hollywood film credits, including Taxi Driver, The Silence of the Lambs and Panic Room, Jodie has turned her hand to directing.

    In the director's chair: The award-winning actress is currently working on 2016 Money Monster which stars George Clooney and Julia Roberts

    Having directed two episodes of Orange Is the New Black and one episode of House of Cards, she's currently working on 2016 Hollywood hit Money Monster which stars George Clooney, Julia Roberts and British-born Jack O'Connell.

    George plays Lee Gates - a TV personality whose insider tips have made him the money guru of Wall Street.

    When Kyle Budwell, played by Jack, loses all of his family's funds on a bad tip, he holds Lee and his entire studio crew hostage on live air and threatens to kill Lee if he doesn't deliver.

    Julia plays a Financial News Network reporter and is also George's romantic interest, a familiar role for the pair as they played lovers in the Ocean's franchise.
