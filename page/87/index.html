Rock Island Police Department announces new K-9

23 Jul 2024, 00:00

The Rock Island Police Department announced its new K-9 officer. According to a release, Blue is the name of the new K-9, which was announced during the March 11 city council meeting. Following a contest in which members of the community were asked to submit names, 1,313 submissions received, and after removing duplicates, 720 unique names had been submitted. The Rock Island Police Department narrowed the list down to 10 names, and Blue was chosen by the children of Officer Alex Bowman, who will again serve as K-9 handler.
