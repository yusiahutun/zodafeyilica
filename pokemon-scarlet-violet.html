    How to Find Dondozo, the False Dragon Titan

    First, you'll need to head to Casseroya Lake. We'd recommend going from Medali and straight across to the water or you can glide down from Glaseado Mountain. This will get you close enough that Arven will call and tell you that no one has seen the False Dragon Titan, and he wishes there was a way for someone to give you a hint.

    advertisement

    Use Koraidon/Miraidon to swim across to the island where the marker is. When you reach the island, you'll find it covered in fish flapping about. But only one, at the southern tip of the island, will be saying "Taitaaan!"

    How to Beat Dondozo, the False Dragon Titan

    Interact with this one and the False Dragon Titan will appear. For the first part of the encounter, you should aim to use Electric or Grass-type moves. Like the other Titan Pokemon, when Dondozo's health has been lowered, it will run away.

    Open your map and find the new marker for the titan. It should be near the central area of the Lake, near shallow water. As you approach the position, you'll jump off of your mount and the Titan will destroy part of the rock.

    Just like the others, it will increase in strength and you'll fight it again. Once it's been defeated, Arven will go to proceed only for the little fish from earlier to appear again and say "Taitaaan!"

    How to Beat Tatsugiri, the False Dragon Titan

    The battle isn't actually over, and it's time for another round - this time against Tatsugiri, the False Dragon Titan. Here, you'll want to switch up the moves you use and start to opt for Dragon and Fairy-type attacks.

    False Dragon Titan Rewards

    When this round is over, Arven will race inside to collect the last Herba Mystica - a Spicy Herba Mystica. Sharing this with Koraidon/Miraidon unlocks the ability to climb up vertical surfaces with your mount. If you press B and jump while you're facing a wall, your mount will now be able to climb the vertical surface.
