    Even though Cillian hasn't publicly addressed the plastic surgery rumours, it is evident that he has had noticeable changes throughout his career. Critics deduce that the actor's tight, wrinkle-free skin in his late forties is unnatural.

    In addition, some of Cillian Murphy's facial changes are due to his dedication to acting. For example, according to Hello Magazine, the actor intentionally lost weight to fit the role of physicist J. Robert Oppenheimer in the 2023 blockbuster Oppenheimer.
