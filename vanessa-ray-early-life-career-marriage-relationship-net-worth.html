    Native of Livermore, California

    Vanessa Ray was born as Vanessa Ray Liptak on June 24, 1981, in Livermore, California, the USA.  She is the daughter of James Liptak and Valerie Liptak. Ray holds an American nationality and belongs to mixed ethnicity.

    Ever since she was a child, Liptak was interested in acting. When she turned 20 she began pursuing her acting career. Vanessa made her fist on-screen debut in 2003 while playing the role of Chris in a youth-oriented short movie called The Sparky Chronicles: The Map. After that, she got many opportunities to work in the entertainment industry.

    She then got a chance to appear in a show called As the World Turns in 2009. From 2013 to 2016, she played the role of CeCe Drake in the series called Pretty Little Liars.  In the same year 2013, she got featured as Eddie Janko in the tv series Blue Bloods alongside, Will Estes, Len Cariou, Sami Gayle, Tom Selleck, & Marisa Ramirez. Later, she portrayed the role of Jenny Griffith in the show called Suits.

    Husband-Landon Beard

    Ray is currently married to her long term boyfriend Landon Beard. The couple first met in 2009 and dated for almost six years. In March 2015, Vanessa finally announced in her social media that she is engaged to her boyfriend Beard. And after three months on June 14, 2015, the couple finally tied their knot and exchanged their wedding vows.

    Their marriage was held at the Condor’s Nest Ranch in eastern Sant Diego, California. In her marriage, She wore a beautiful two-piece dress with spaghetti strap top and long skirt. Whereas for Beard, he wore a plain sky blue suit and pants with a printed tie and printed flower shirt.

    Looking at their wedding picture, we can see that the couple looks so happy with each other. In the ceremony, the couple invited most of their relatives, friends, and family.  For now, the two has already celebrated their 4 years anniversary. Yet, the couple hasn’t plan for a baby.

    Ex-husband Derek James Baynham

    Prior to her relationship with Beard, Vanessa was previously married to Derek James Baynham on March 23, 2003. Derek is an actor, director, and producer and he is best known for appearing in movies like Mulligans, OMG!, and Darla. Ray and Baynham’s marriage was also shown in a reality show called Marriage Under Construction.

    However, their marriage didn’t last long, and their marriage turns into a divorce after six years of their marriage life.  Ray and Baynham finally take the decision and officially got the divorce in 2009. After that, the couple parts their way whereas in-between their marriage the couple didn’t share any children.
