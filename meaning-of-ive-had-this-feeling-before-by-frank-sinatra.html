    Meaning of Ive Had This Feeling Before by Frank Sinatra

    09 Oct 2024, 00:00

    The overall theme of Frank Sinatra's song "I've Had This Feeling Before" centers around the powerful impact that love can have on a person's life. Through the lyrics, Sinatra explores the intensity and unexpected nature of falling in love, highlighting both the joy and vulnerability that comes with such an experience.One standout lyric that helps develop this theme is "Love had me reeling before but never like this." Here, Sinatra acknowledges that while he may have experienced love in the past, the love he is currently experiencing is unlike anything he has ever felt before. This lyric explores the idea of love as an ever-evolving emotion, constantly surprising and surpassing previous experiences. It underscores the transformative power of love, suggesting that it has the ability to completely change someone's perspective and feelings.Another important lyric in the song is "I must have really been napping, to let you walk off with my heart." This lyric adds a sense of vulnerability to the song, as Sinatra reflects on his own momentary lapse in judgment that led him to fall in love. It suggests that love can catch us off guard, blindsiding even the most self-assured individuals. The lyric communicates a sense of surrender and surrendering control to the overwhelming emotions associated with love.Sinatra's lyrics also touch on the idea of dreams and their connection to love. He sings, "My dreams, and I had quite a few, were never like this, This is too good to be true, but so was that kiss." This lyric explores the notion that falling in love can often feel like a dream come true, surpassing any expectations one may have had. It conveys a sense of enchantment and wonder, suggesting that love can make one feel as if they are living in a perfect, surreal world.Furthermore, Sinatra's lyrics delve into the concept of taking risks in love. He muses, "That this time I'm playing for keeps." This lyric suggests that love is not always guaranteed, but when it feels right, it is worth fighting for. It touches on the importance of commitment and embracing the uncertainties that come with love. By expressing his willingness to play for keeps, Sinatra showcases the transformative power of love and the strength it gives individuals to overcome fear and take chances.In conclusion, Frank Sinatra's song "I've Had This Feeling Before" delves into the emotional and transformative journey of falling in love. Through introspective and heartfelt lyrics, Sinatra explores themes of vulnerability, surrender, dreams, and risk-taking. The song captures the essence of love's ability to surprise, inspire, and change a person's life in ways they never imagined.
