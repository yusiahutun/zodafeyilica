    EJ Johnson reveals he despises his sisters boyfriend who led him on in episode of EJNYC

    Diva EJ Johnson is less than impressed with his sisters boyfriend claiming he flirted with him outrageously then left him hanging. EJ, the 24-year-old son of basketball legend Magic Johnson, got into a spat with his younger sister Elisa over her new beau Anthony a French model.

    08 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Diva EJ Johnson is less than impressed with his sister’s boyfriend – claiming he flirted with him outrageously then left him hanging.

    EJ, the 24-year-old son of basketball legend Magic Johnson, got into a spat with his younger sister Elisa over her new beau Anthony – a French model.

    EJ claimed that his sister’s new boyfriend had led him on in a club, before they’d got together.

    Big debut: EJ Johnson's new reality show EJ NYC premiered on E! on Sunday night

    ‘He’s a loser,’ he raged during the premier of his new reality show EJNYC.

    ‘I was told not to say anything about him kissing me, but he did, it happened,’ he griped.

    ‘The man that led me on is now hooking up with my sister, and I’m supposed to be OK with it’.

    Elisa had already celebrated her 21st birthday in The Bahamas.

    Dynamic duo: Also starring on the programme with his sister Elisa

    ‘But now my parents are going to throw me an even bigger party, in New York,’ she squealed.

    The soiree meant EJ and Anthony meeting for the first time since EJ was jilted.

    ‘I will never like him,’ said EJ, telling Elisa that she should not have continued a relationship with Anthony after ‘everything that happened’.

    ‘It’s like my feelings don't matter,’ he told her petulantly.

    ‘My brother wants me to cut things off with Anthony, but I can’t do that because I have developed serious feelings for him,’ she said.

    Take me out to the ballgame: EJ and his sister took in a Los Angeles Dodgers baseball game as their famous father Magic Johnson is part of the ownership group of the team

    Sibling rivalry: There is a conflict in their relationship as they got into a spat over her new boyfriend Anthony – a French model

    Before the party EJ harped with his friends about how Anthony had teased him in order to ‘pop bottles’ with his wealthy crew.

    ‘Social climbing for beginners,’ chimed in one of his male chums.

    In the midst of the drama EJ met with his mother Cookie Johnson for lunch in Manhattan.

    ‘When you’re here you are your own person…you’re not in your father’s shadow,’ she told him.

    Cookie, who has a denim line, suggested that he ‘collaborate with someone’ for his first clothing collection.

    Shade: EJ claimed that his sister's new boyfriend had led him on in a club while on holiday

    Harsh words: The Rich Kids Of Beverly Hills star was none-too-pleased as he described Anthony as 'a loser'

    She also advised that he make amends with Anthony and his sister.

    ‘Blood is thicker than water,’ she told him.

    EJ decided that he was going to make a name for himself, without any connections to his father.

    ‘I’m not just somebody’s son,’ he said.

    Before the party Anthony became jealous that Elisa had invited her ex boyfriend and ‘first love’ DJ.

    Jetset: Shortly after his arrival in New York, EJ vented to his pals about the situation

    'Social climing for beginners': EJ harped with his friends about how Anthony had teased him in order to ‘pop bottles’ with his wealthy crew.

    ‘That’s crazy, I’m not seeing my ex,’ he told her.

    ‘There’s so much drama that is attached to this relationship, I don’t even know if it’s worth it,’ sighed Elisa, who is best friends with LL Cool J’s daughter Samaria.

    As they shopped for wigs they discussed the problems associated with having famous parents.

    ‘Growing up, a lot of people would ask me if my last name was Cool J,’ said Samaria incredulously.

    Elisa wanted her brother to accept Anthony: ‘I have to have my family’s approval,’ she sobbed.

    Fashionista EJ wore a skintight catsuit to the rendevouz.

    ‘I have to have my family’s approval’: Elisa was worried about the entire situation

    ‘I’m Catwoman…looking bomb, doing the absolute most,’ he cooed, before deciding that he would ‘be cordial’ to Anthony.

    ‘But he will be going out of our apartment, affective immediately,’ he stipulated.

    Elisa went gooey to see her ex, DJ, arrive.

    ‘It’s a feeling of positivity…I’m so fricking happy you came,’ she told him.

    Anthony got extremely jealous when he saw them together.

    ‘Go to him…I don’t give a f*ck,’ he cursed angrily.

    ‘This is a disaster,’ she concluded.
