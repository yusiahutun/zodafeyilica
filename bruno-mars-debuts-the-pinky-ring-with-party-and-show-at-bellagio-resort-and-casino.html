    Bruno Mars Debuts The Pinky Ring With Party And Show At Bellagio Resort And Casino

    Bruno Mars and the Hooligans, The Pinky Ring

    LAS VEGAS (CelebrityAccess) – Global superstar Bruno Mars set off the night on Saturday (February 10), opening the doors to his Las Vegas party destination and entertainment lounge – The Pinky Ring – inside Bellagio Resort and Casino with an exclusive celebration and performance.

    Stepping into the chic venue designed by Mars were a variety of his friends, including Janelle Monáe, Lady Gaga, Victoria Monét, Babyface, T-Pain, Gayle King, Jason and Lauren Kennedy, Kerrueche Tran, and Lucky Daye – amongst others – some of whom joined Mars onstage to perform.

    The party kicked off with a high-energy performance from Mars and his longtime band, The Hooligans. The band played several hits, including “That’s What I Like” and “Treasure,” along with some classic covers that matched the mood of the intimate lounge, such as The Jackson 5’s “Shake Your Body (Down to the Ground),” Junior Senior’s “Move Your Feet” and Bob Marley’s “Is This Love.”

    Guests enjoyed surprise performances from some of Bruno’s talented friends—including Monáe, Babyface, Daye and Monét.

    The Pinky Ring opens to the public on Monday (February 12), with The Hooligans featured as the resident house band for the first two weeks. Guests will enjoy Mars’ homage to the entertainers who helped shape Las Vegas and immerse themselves in his vision of timeless glamour and remarkable artistry. Every element of the experience—from the specialty bar program and live entertainment—has been curated by the global superstar as a place for people to get lost in a good time they will remember forever.
