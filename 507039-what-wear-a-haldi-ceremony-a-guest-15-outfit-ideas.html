    What to wear to a Haldi ceremony as a guest is a subject that often arises when preparing to attend this special event. Several traditional attires will stand out when you wear them at a Haldi ceremony. Women can do sarees or lehenga cholis in vibrant colours, while men can opt for traditional kurta pyjamas or more formal sherwanis.

    Haldi refers to turmeric, a bright yellow spice with cultural and traditional significance in Indian customs. The Haldi ceremony is a pre-wedding ritual commonly practised in Indian weddings. It is a joyful and festive event, and dressing in traditional Indian clothing or outfits that complement the celebratory atmosphere is highly recommended.

    What to wear to a Haldi ceremony as a guest

    What do female guests wear to an Indian wedding? Choosing the right attire is essential to fully embrace the Haldi ceremony's cultural significance and celebratory atmosphere. Female guests typically wear colorful traditional Indian attire such as sarees, lehengas, or Sharara pants to this event.

    On the other hand, male guests can wear simple and comfortable traditional outfits such as kurta pyjama or dhoti kurta. The color of the outfit can vary but light or yellow colors are preferred.

    15 best outfit ideas to wear to a Haldi ceremony as a guest

    It is always good to check with the hosts or the couple for any specific dress code or guidelines they may have for the Haldi ceremony. Here are some outfits you can wear to a Haldi ceremony:

    1. Printed saree

    A printed saree refers to a type of traditional Indian garment known as a saree that features various patterns, motifs, or designs printed onto the fabric. It is a popular choice among women for various occasions, including weddings, festivals, and formal events.

    2. Kanjeevaram silk saree

    A Kanjeevaram silk saree, also known as Kanchipuram silk saree, is a luxurious and traditional Indian garment known for its exquisite craftsmanship and rich silk fabric. It originates from Kanchipuram in Tamil Nadu, India, and is highly regarded for its beauty and cultural significance. Kanjeevaram silk saree is one of the best saree for wedding guest.

    3. Banaras silk saree

    Worry no more if you are wondering what to wear at a wedding as a guest or woman. A Banaras silk saree will do you justice on such occasions. The intricate weaving technique, opulent designs, and rich heritage associated with their creation set Banaras silk sarees apart. It is often handcrafted in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India city, a silk weaving centre for centuries.

    4. Lehenga choli

    Wearing a Lehenga choli at a wedding ceremony is a popular and appropriate choice. Lehenga choli is a traditional Indian outfit that consists of a long skirt (lehenga), a fitted blouse (choli), and a dupatta (scarf). It is a glamorous and elegant ensemble often worn by women at weddings and other festivals.

    5. Sharara pants with short kurtis

    A sharara comprises wide-legged pants paired with a short kurta and a dupatta. This ensemble combines traditional and contemporary elements, making it perfect for a Haldi ceremony.

    What are appropriate colors for an Indian wedding as a guest?

    As a guest attending an Indian wedding, a wide range of colours is considered appropriate and suitable. Vibrant colours are often preferred to celebrate the joyous atmosphere. Traditional colours like yellow, red, maroon, and gold are popular choices, symbolising prosperity and auspiciousness.

    Other colours like royal blue, green, deep purple, and hot pink are well-received. However, it is important to avoid wearing white, as it is traditionally associated with mourning in Indian culture.
