    Call us paranoid, but lately, we’ve noticed that there are a lot of male celebrities who now appear to have more hair on their heads than before. No, we’re not talking about the men who changed their styles or grew their hair out. We’re talking about guys with bald scalps that miraculously started to grow hair again — or in other words, famous guys who probably got hair transplants.

    GET MORE: See Kelly Ripa, Faith Hill and 10 More of the Most Drastic Celebrity Hair Transformations

    Believe it or not, hair is extremely important to men. It’s basically their crown and glory — just like it is for most women!

    But in most cases, it’s not until they start to lose it that they realize how important their hair is to them. In fact, stars like Elton John have revealed that they’re not afraid to go the extra mile to keep a full head of hair.

    “I’m glad I had them because before I had no hair at all. It just astounds me how people are so interested in it,” he once said.

    Another celeb, Calum Best, hit the nail on the head when he opened up about what it’s like to experience baldness.

    “There is no doubt the [hair transplants] have changed my life. No man wants to be bald when he is older. I have been losing my hair since my early 20s. Nature’s plan was for me to have thin and patchy hair across the top of my scalp. But the great thing is, we don’t have to follow nature’s plan,” he said.

    We couldn’t agree more!
