    06 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Memorial Day weekend is finally upon us, and as eager as we are to have a long weekend and kick off the summer season, we must first reflect on the history of Memorial Day. While you might be focused on your barbecue or beach trip, take time to honor those who bravely gave their lives serving our country. (If you need an extra reminder of the importance of the holiday, be sure to check out our inspiring Memorial Day quotes.)

    Of course, the federal holiday does give nearly everyone a long weekend, and you should definitely take advantage of the extra day off by checking out our Memorial Day activities. If you'd rather not take a big trip to the grocery store and fire up the grill, fear not! There are plenty of restaurants open on Memorial Day. Here are the eating establishments staying open on Monday, May 29.

    Restaurants Open on Memorial Day 2023:

    Locations are open for dine-in and carryout of their taste of the Islands menu. Pick up a family-style menu for four with entrées like baby back ribs, jerk chicken pasta, or seafood paella, or get a table on the deck and enjoy a tropical cocktail, like a Bahama Mama or a Pineapple-Coconut Martini.

    Bob Evans restaurants will be open during regular hours on Memorial Day. Try the new country-fried steak platter or, for a lighter option, one of the farm-fresh summer salads.

    All locations are offering specials for Memorial Day weekend. On Memorial Day and every day, service members, veterans, and first responders receive 10% off their order. Simply show your ID to get the discount.

    Boston Market restaurants are open on May 29. Check with the location nearest you for specific hours and specials.

    Buffalo Wild Wings restaurants will operate under normal business hours. Call ahead to order a family bundle of 20 bone-in and 20 boneless wings and fries to feed the crew.

    Every day of the year, military service members and first responders receive 10% off at Carrabba’s.

    All of The Cheesecake Factory locations will be open under normal business hours with pickup, delivery, and curbside to-go options.

    Locations are offering family bundles this Memorial Day. Choose from entrées like chicken tenders and grilled salmon, along with sides and their signature Honey Butter Croissants.

    Thinking about some baby back ribs to kick off summer? Head to Chili's! Locations are open for dine-in, take-out, and pickup. Feeding a crowd? Order a party platter of their mini burgers or Southwestern egg rolls. You can also pick up a margarita kit to-go where available.

    The restaurant is offering all-new catering bundles just in time for summer. Choose from sirloin steak tips, cheeseburger sliders, or sirloin steak tips n' tenders. Add seasonal sides like corn on the cob and and refreshing strawberry lemonade.

    Denny's restaurants will be open. Feed the family with a breakfast, lunch, or dinner family pack.

    Try the special burger and wine menu at the steakhouse (available May 26-29). Choose from the prime burger, California burger, or crispy chickpea and eggplant burger with a glass of Flowers Pinot Noir for $30.

    The Brazilian steakhouse is open on Memorial Day. Contact your local restaurant for specifics.

    The buffet will have a new offering beginning May 22: baby back ribs, chicken tenders, and watermelon. Select locations have a special breakfast buffet on Memorial Day.

    IHOP is open on Memorial Day. Hours vary by location. Check out their family bundles offered for delivery and curbside pickup.

    Give your grill master the day off this year and pick up steaks to go at Longhorn.

    From May 25-29, pick up two pizzas (Margherita, Pepperoni, Sausage & Pepperoni, BBQ Chicken or Newk's "Q) and a salad (Caesar, Greek or Garden) for $20.

    All Noodles & Company locations will be open for takeout, delivery, and curbside pickup, though hours do vary by location, so make sure you check your local restaurant's hours before heading out.

    All locations are open on May 29. Contact your local restaurant for details.

    Outback Steakhouse is open to feed your steak craving this Memorial Day.

    Yes, you can get your Chicken Lettuce Wraps even on Memorial Day. P.F. Chang's will be open during normal business hours.

    Red Lobster restaurants will be open during normal hours throughout Memorial Day weekend. Check the website for your location's dining options.

    Select locations of the steakhouse will be open on Memorial Day. Go online to find specifics about the restaurant close to you.

    You don't even have to get out of your car to get a yummy snack from Sonic. All locations will be open, but because nearly all of them are locally owned, hours do vary. Be sure to check in advance before driving up.

    Need a little pick-me-up? Starbucks is staying open, but hours vary by location, so double-check before you go out for a cup of joe.

    Head to Ted's for American-raised bison, steaks, chicken, or seafood, or a delicious burger. Save time by making a reservation or joining the waitlist online.

    Dine in or pick up a family platter of wings, ribs, appetizers, and desserts to feed a crowd.
