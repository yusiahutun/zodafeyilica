A tattoo artist reveals the grisly method behind his body art: he slices off the top layer of skin and paints the wound with ink. Yann Brenyak, from London, removes a layer of skin with a scalpel in the shape of the design and then fills the pattern with ink in a process that he calls 'scarification.' Yann specialises in body modification and carves all sorts of shapes from portraits to skeletons in a gory style that has earned him over 16,000 followers on Instagram.
