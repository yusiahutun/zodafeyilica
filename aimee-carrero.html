    Delightful Wedding With Her Long Term Boyfriend

    Elena of Avalor star Aimee Carrero is happily living a joyous married life with her boyfriend turned husband Tim Rock, an American actor, and writer.

    Aimee and Tim beautifully captured in a photograph. SOURCE: Instagram: @aimeecarrero

    The duo united as life partners on August 20, 2016, and cherished a destination wedding ceremony.

    The pair picked the majestic mountains of Starpeak Estate in Aspen, Colorado, for their breathtaking wedding day celebration. It was 10 miles up away from the Aspen city.

    Beautiful majestic mountains in Aspen. SOURCE: Yahoo News

    Wedding planner of Bluebird Productions highlighted the alpine mountains during a vow taking ceremony where family, friends, and relatives were seated in a semi-circle platform facing the majestic backdrop. A 200 ft wooden walkway was built where Aimee and Tim walked down the aisle. The semi-circle platform was adorned with white rose petals.

    The 31-year-old Aimee recalled,

    Even though neither of us was particularly 'outdoorsy,' we knew we wanted to be connected to nature on the day we made the most important commitment of our lives. We also knew we wanted our guests to feel like they had been dropped into a Walden-esque setting.

    The focus part of the ceremony was a living wall made by the florist Prema Style, where it was written 'Love on the rocks.' It was the slogan and hashtag for the wedding weekend.

    The hashtag for the wedding weekend. SOURCE: yahoo

    For the big day, the bride dressed in an intricated embroiders and floral appliqued long white veil with lace, by Naeem Khan. Guiseppe Zanotti shoes and diamond earrings co-ordinated her elegant appearance. Aimee looked so beautiful, making her hair down in loose waves and nude make up look.

    Aimee in her beautiful wedding attire. SOURCE: Harper'sbazaar

    On the other hand, the groom appeared in a custom suit by Thom Browne.

    Tim with other Groomsmen in a custom suit. SOURCE: Yahoo News

    D'elissious Cake Studio helped the couple with an elegant asymmetric wedding cake that featured a simple gold leaf and a single sugar flower on it.

    The Wedding Cake. SOURCE: Harper'sbazaar

    The wedding celebration furthermore went throughout the night, in the outdoor setting. There was wooden furniture covered with light-colored table cloths and candles on top of it with additional decorations. The romantic closing gave a rustic feel to all the attendance.

    For the wedding night, the bride appeared in a custom white jumpsuit and feather cape by Annabelle Haron and Stuart Weitzman sandals.

    Wedding night celebration of Aimee and Tim. SOURCE: Rachel Havel photography

    A newlywed couple is always surprised by amazing wedding gifts. Similarly, Aimee and Tim also received an unexpected wedding gift from a stranger which they found near their wedding venue.

    Along with the gift, they also found a letter, and there was written;
