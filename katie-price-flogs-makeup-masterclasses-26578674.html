    Katie Price flogs makeup masterclasses for 80 on Instagram

    Katie Price has announced she's heading out on tour to dish out her top makeup tips to fans. The former Page 3 model knows a thing or two about how to achieve glamorous looks, so now she's decided to share her beauty wisdom with the world.

    25 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Katie Price has announced she's heading out on tour to dish out her top makeup tips to fans.

    The former Page 3 model knows a thing or two about how to achieve glamorous looks, so now she's decided to share her beauty wisdom with the world.

    The ex-glamour star took to her Instagram Stories on Monday (March 28) to reveal her "makeup masterclasses" which she's flogging for £80 each.

    Katie's exciting summer tour this year will take her around the UK to cities like London, Birmingham and Scotland's Falkirk as well as Limerick in Ireland.

    The makeup classes will also see the mum-of-five team up with makeup artist Fern Howe-Shepherd.

    Fans looking to snap up tickets will have to act fast though as some dates, such as Cardiff and Middlesbrough, are already sold out.

    Talking to her fans, Katie said: "My makeup masterclasses are back this summer in the UK & Ireland with @fern_makeup".

    Promoting tickets via a link to her website, she added: "Tap here for tickets to see me".

    On her website, Katie Price Merch, tour information explained each ticket comes with a chance to meet Katie, take photos with her and as her questions during the class.

    Fans will also walk away with a goody bag worth £20 on the day.

    The website read: "Join Katie Price for a day filled with makeup!

    "Katie Price will share her top makeup looks and explain how to recreate them alongside her makeup artist Fern Howe Shepherd using her favourite makeup products and own makeup line.

    "Everyone will get a chance to meet Katie & take a photo with her and ask her questions during the masterclass.

    "You will get a Goody bag worth £20 on the day."

    Katie recently questioned why she hadn't been offered an MBE as she claimed "others have done less" to get them in the past.

    The TV star has been tirelessly campaigning for stricter laws on trolling after her eldest son Harvey was targeted with abuse by cruel trolls.

    Speaking to The Guardian, Katie said: "I’m in the Houses of Parliament campaigning for better laws and opening up people’s eyes to life for people with disabilities. Honestly, I see others getting MBEs who’ve done less than me, and I think: why not me?"
