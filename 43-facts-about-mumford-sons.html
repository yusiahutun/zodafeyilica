    Mumford & Sons formed in London in 2007. The band consists of Marcus Mumford (lead vocals, guitar, drums), Ben Lovett (vocals, keyboard, accordion), Winston Marshall (vocals, banjo, guitar), and Ted Dwane (vocals, double bass, guitar).

    Mumford & Sons is known for their blend of folk, rock, and alternative sounds. They incorporate elements of bluegrass and Americana into their music as well.

    Some of Mumford & Sons’ most popular songs include “I Will Wait,” “Little Lion Man,” “The Cave,” “Believe,” and “Lover of the Light.”

    Mumford & Sons has released four studio albums: “Sigh No More” (2009), “Babel” (2012), “Wilder Mind” (2015), and “Delta” (2018).
