    Ruth Wilson is an English actress. She is known for her role as the protagonist of the same name in Jane Eyre, as Alice Morgan in the BBC crime drama Luther, as Alison Lockhart in the Showtime The Affair television series and the main character of Ms. Wilson. Since 2019 she has played the role of Marisa Coulter in the BBC / HBO fantasy series His Dark Materials and for this role she won the 2020 BAFTA Cymru Award for Best Actress. His film credits include The Lone Ranger, Saving Mr. Banks, I'm the Pretty Thing That Lives in the House, and Dark River. Wilson is a three-time Olivier Award nominee and two-time award winner, winning Best Actress for her lead role in Anna Christie and Best Supporting Actress for her role as Stella Kowalski in A Streetcar Named Desire.
