    Hailey Bieber is keeping the strawberry girl summer theme going! Her latest red dress and matching heels made the Internet swoon as the Rhode Skin founder appeared for her new Strawberry Glaze launch.

    Justin Bieber is also promoting the launch through his own Instagram as he uploaded a couple of snaps of hers in the Donut chain with a sweet caption that read: “baby girl with the @rhode @krispykreme STRAWBERRY GLAZEEE.”

    Hailey Bieber heads to Krispy Kreme in a strawberry-red dress

    Hailey Bieber stunned the internet in a berry dress for the Krispy Kreme event. She paired her off-the-shoulder Vivienne Westwood midi dress with matching peep-toe pumps. Hailey’s latest look promotes her new Rhode launch, a limited edition of her peptide lip treatment, a sheer berry red gloss inspired by Krispy Kreme’s strawberry glaze donut better than anything else could.

    But this isn’t the first time Hailey is channeling a berry red look for Summer. The model recently donned a Magda Butrym halterneck dress in a fiery scarlet shade starting the trend of strawberry girl summer.

    TikTok is not over strawberry girl summer

    Hailey took over TikTok with her laid-back strawberry-girl summer look and since then, users of the platform have been keeping the trend alive. After the early summer’s vanilla girl and latte girl aesthetic, it is Mrs. Bieber’s strawberry looks that beauty influencers are using as inspiration.

    Rhodes product that influencers love for the ultimate strawberry girl look

    Hailey’s beauty brand Rhodes features a number of fruity products that go well with the current summer trend. Her original Rhode Strawberry Peptide Lip Treatment is a staple to achieve the strawberry girl look which is now available in a new limited strawberry glaze edition. Other shades of the range include watermelon slice, salted caramel, vanilla, and an unscented version for the ones who are sensitive to any scent in their skincare.
