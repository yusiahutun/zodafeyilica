    QC entries win in Hereford Show at Iowa State Fair

    Young Cattle Company of Lexington, Ill., took home Grand Champion Horned Female in the Hereford Show judged at the 2022 Iowa State Fair. Tyler Davisson of Calamus received Reserve Champion Horned Female and Champion Iowa Horned Female.

    12 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Young Cattle Company of Lexington, Ill., took home Grand Champion Horned Female in the Hereford Show judged at the 2022 Iowa State Fair.

    Tyler Davisson of Calamus received Reserve Champion Horned Female and Champion Iowa Horned Female.

    Tjardes Farms of Foosland, Ill., claimed Grand Champion Bull. Young Cattle Company received Reserve Grand Champion Bull.

    Putz Cattle & Working Aussies of Otley earned Champion Iowa Bull.
