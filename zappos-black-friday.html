    While part of our Black Friday shopping plan includes picking up things like stocking stuffers and celeb-approved skincare products, another part is all about adding to our wardrobe. The Zappos Black Friday sale, for example, is packed full of stuff we’ve been lusting after for months: cute loafers, white sneakers, relaxed denim, everyday bags, etc. We can’t — and won’t! — miss out on great deals like these. After all, they won’t last forever.

    Don’t worry, though. We’ll of course follow the “one for you, one for me” shopping rule, scanning Zappos’ Black Friday sale for treats for ourselves and presents for everyone else. The designers discounts are aplenty, and we could easily fill our virtual cart with a bunch of shoes, skirts, and accessories for well under $200.

    Zappos, thankfully, is among the group of stores rolling out Early Black Friday sales, which means prices are already marked down and pieces are going quickly. So what’s worth buying from the Zappos sale before it’s too late? Check out our list of must-haves, ahead.

    • Kate Spade New York Crystal Embellished Tote

      $243.6034830% Off

      We know how versatile black bags can be, but the embellishments on this mini tote add a little something special. Gift it to a pal this holiday season or snag it for yourself to add sparkle to your outfits. Either way, the small yet spacious design will no doubt be cherished — and receive a ton of compliments.

    • Madden Girl Viviee Boots

      $53.416923% Off

      If you’ve ever wondered what to wear with wide-leg jeans and trousers, a chunky combat boot is the answer. Madden Girl’s platform pair will balance out proportions and toughen up your look. Plus, reviews say they’re comfortable and easy to walk in.

    • Madewell Skinny Flare Jeans

      $96.6013830% Off

      Madewell has a reputation for making the cutest, most comfortable jeans and Zappos is selling a pair for less than $100. If you’re a skinny jean lover who wants to update their look without going too baggy, this slim-fitting flare is the perfect in-between style.

    • MICHAEL Michael Kors Rue Stiletto Boot

      $112.7527559% Off

      Taylor Swift, is that you? That’s what people will be asking once you slip these “Bejeweled” babies on. They’re super similar to the ones the singer wears on stage, but (thankfully) cost a fraction of the price.

    • UNTUCKit Trieste Shirt

      $69.309930% Off

      Guys, forget fussing with your shirt all day or wondering how to style it. This plaid button-down is short enough to be worn untucked (hence the brand name) and is also a “wrinkle free” design, so you won’t have to worry about steaming or ironing it, either.

    • Free People Teagan Tassel Loafer

      $81.4014845% Off

      Loafers are one shoe trend that doesn’t seem to be dying down. For less than $100, you can gift someone a cute wardrobe staple beloved by style icons such as Hailey Bieber and Kendall Jenner. And if that someone is yourself? Well, we won’t judge.

    • Vince Camuto Puntolis Heels

      $83.2511930% Off

      It’s something we never realize we need until we need it: a basic black heel. Sure, you could risk it by ordering a dupe, which will no doubt leave your feet aching by the end of the night. Or, you could snag this quality Vince Camuto pair now for a fraction of its original price.

    • Show Me Your Mumu Angel Maxi Dress

      $100.8617843% Off

      Considering how versatile the Angel Maxi Dress is, we’re kind of surprised it’s not already sold out. It works for almost every occasion: vacation, lounging around the house, a summer date night, etc. Brides-to-be will also appreciate the white colorway.

    • Coach Elsie Leather Wedge

      $7525070% Off

      Coach may be know for its bags, but don’t ignore its selection of shoes (or apparel for that matter). Zappos put these sweet leather wedges on sale for Black Friday and now, we’re counting down the days until summer so that we can rock them.

    • Adidas Originals Stan Smith

      $7010030% Off

      Adidas’ most classic styles are everywhere again and the Stan Smith is no exception. We’re personally digging the purple details on this pair, which add a little flair to an otherwise neutral design.

    • UGG Edison Jacket

      $75.1511836% Off

      Zappos has plenty of UGG on sale for Black Friday, but among the bunch is a sleek, black bomber jacket that would make a great gift for the men on your list. It’s so versatile and bound to become their new go-to.

    • Kendra Scott Elle Enamel Framed Drop Earrings

      $37.507550% Off

      Somewhere out there, a mom or sister is eyeing these shiny Kendra Scott drop earrings. If you’re struggling to find a gift and don’t want to spend a ton of dough, this pair cost just $37.50, which is super reasonable given how glam they are.

    • Mango Gala Skirt

      $69.999929% Off

      Were you aware that Mango — a brand that Gigi Hadid, Emily Ratajkowski, and Kaia Gerber all love — was sold on Zappos? We’re breaking out our credit card immediately and purchasing a mid-length Gala skirt, which will no doubt look super cute styled with tights and a knit sweater.

    • Levi's Ex-Boyfriend Sherpa Trucker

      $87.9110819% Off

      Aside from classic pairs of jeans, Levi’s makes the denim jackets of our dreams. Case in point: this warm sherpa design, which comes in two colors (white and blue) and might be one of the best deals of the year, consider how often we know we (or the person we’re buying it for) will wear this thing.

    • Madewell Plus Poplin Halter Tiered Midi Dress

      $78.4414847% Off

      We know, we know — Madewell’s on this list twice. But we couldn’t leave out this flowy, tiered, black midi dress, which not only works for weddings (when paired with heels) but also warm, casual days (when paired with sandals or sneakers).

    • Puma Suede Classic XXI Sneakers

      $52.507530% Off

      When the rest of your outfit is somewhat simple, nothing beats amping it up with a colorful shoe. These sneakers are a true conversation starter and will help to balance out a wardrobe full of black, white, and other neutral shades.

    • Spanx Seamless Leggings

      $39.606842% Off

      If your leggings collection is full of basic black options, switch things up by adding this slightly more elaborate pair to the mix. It includes a double waistband, so you’ll feel extra supported while you do your thing, whether that’s walking on the treadmill or running errands.

    • Sorel Hi-Line Hiker Boot

      $90.6318551% Off

      If your goal for the next year is to spend more time in nature, you’re going to need to dress the part, too. Hiking boots will especially come in handy for long treks, and if anyone knows how to make a shoe built for outdoor action, it’s Sorel.
