    08 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Rose Ayling-Ellis could make an eye-watering £13k per month following her exit from EastEnders, according to new data.

    The Strictly Come Dancing star announced earlier this year that she would be saying goodbye to the role of Frankie Carter in the BBC soap, after more than two years.

    However new data gathered by HypeAuditor, exclusively for The Daily Star, shows that Rose could make a huge sum from brand deals and advertisements following her EastEnders exit.

    The tool found that Rose has a good audience quality and good engagement quality on Instagram, indicating that her 542.3k followers are "legitimate users."

    They said: "the comments and likes she receives are given by legitimate followers, that are not bots or fake accounts."

    As such, her Instagram account has a good engagement rate, meaning Rose could expect to charge brands she is partnering with between £2,560 to £3355 per post, and £618 to £882 per story.

    With Rose estimated to post on her Instagram account once per week on average, it seems that the star could make anywhere between £10,240 to £13,420 per month from brand deals and advertisements on social media alone.

    HypeAuditor also commented that Rose's "monthly increase in terms of number of followers is low."

    Her Instagram following looks set to keep increasing too, after Rose confirmed her first new project since leaving the show.

    She's currently taking on her first Shakespeare role in As You Like It.

    Announcing her retirement from the role of Frankie, Rose said: "It's been incredibly special to be EastEnders' first regular deaf actor.

    "I have loved my time on the show and Frankie has been such an awesome character to play, however now feels like the right time for me to move on and I am excited and ready for new challenges."

    It is possible that dancing could be on the cards, after she and Strictly Come Dancing partner Giovanni Pernice were snapped at his sister Angela's dance studio in Sicily.

    It is known that she and Giovanni earned a jaw-dropping sum of £100,000 for winning Strictly, as well as going on to take part in the Strictly tour, which can earn participants an extra £35,000, according to reports.

    It is also thought that Rose's current net worth is estimated at a huge £370,000 following her Strictly win, according to BiographyDaily.

    So could a future in dance be the next step in Rose's future? Fans will have to wait and see.
