      Maroon 5 frontman Adam Levine is engaged to Victoria's Secret model Behati Prinsloo.The 34-year-old singer popped the question to the Namibian beauty in Los Angeles last weekend.

      His representative confirmed the happy news to People, saying, “Adam Levine and his girlfriend Behati Prinsloo are excited to announce they are engaged to be married,”The news came as a surprise to some fans — last month Adam was spotted on holiday with Sports Illustrated Danish model Nina Agdal in Mexico.

      But he recently reconciled with Namibian beauty Behati, after splitting with her earlier this year.

      "He realised he really does love her and she was the one, " a source told . "They are in love. "The couple began dating in May 2012, shortly after The Voice judge split from swimsuit model Anne Vyalitsyna after two years of dating.

      Adam recently admitted he would love to settle down. “I'm a fan of marriage. People think that I keep pooh-poohing marriage, but I love it. I want to probably be married at some point,” he said.“I don't feel pressure to get married. I think that when it's time, it's going to be obvious and I'm going to do it and I'm going to really enjoy it,” he added.

      The Maroon 5 lead singer has dated several high-profile celebrities including singer Jessica Simpson and tennis champion Maria Sharapova.He currently stars as a judge on NBC’s hit show, The Voice, alongside Shakira, Usher and Blake Shelton.
