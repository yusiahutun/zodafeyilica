    • She paired her sleeveless frock with knee-high leather boots, black sunglasses, a mini Louis Vuitton handbag, and gold-tone jewelry.

    The pouring rain in New York City won't stop Kendall Jenner from fully embracing summer style. The model and reality TV star was snapped on the streets of SoHo in a leopard print mini dress with a smocked bodice from eco-friendly line Reformation.

    Jenner's exact dress retails just shy of $200 and is unfortunately sold out. But thankfully, Reformation–a line adored by EmRata, Karlie Kloss (who's also an investor), and Duchess Meghan Markle–has a similar off-the-shoulder option and knee-length version that are both available to shop right now.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    Kendall accessorized her flirty and carefree look with a surprising pair of knee-high black leather boots and rectangular black sunglasses. She completed her laid-back fit with a few luxe elements including gold mini hoop earrings, a chain-link bracelet, and a Louis Vuitton monogram pochette from the Stephen Sprouse collection.

    Jenner appears to be a fan of animal print garments from Reformation. Before she wore a meme-worthy dress to the Met Gala, Jenner was photographed in a black and white zebra print blouse with leather pants by the brand. At the time, she added mini gold-tone hoop earrings and black Simon Miller sandals to her look.
