    Two-time Olympic gold medalist footballer, Carli Lloyd is well known as a midfielder for Sky Blue FC in the National Women’s Soccer League. She has made over 280 appearances for the U.S. national team and has the 4th most goals and seventh-most assists for the team. She became the first player ever to score a hattrick in a FIFA Women’s World Cup final.

    Lloyd is a married woman as of 2019. She is living a happy married life with her lovely husband. So, does she share any children from her marriage with her spouse? Explore more about Lloyd’s personal life below.

    Who Is Carli Anne Hollins? Bio & Wiki

    Carli Lloyd was born as Carli Anne Hollins on 16th July 1982, in Delran Township, New Jersey, United States. Moreover, she holds American nationality and her ethnic background is Caucasian white. Lloyd was raised by her father, Stephen and her mother, Pamela Lloyd. She grew up with her younger brother, Stephen and her sister, Ashley.

    Lloyd started playing football at the age of five. From 1997 to 2000, she attended Delran High School where she trained under Rudy “The Red Baron” Klobach. Later, she enrolled with a scholarship at Rutgers University from 2001 to 2004.

    While studying at Rutgers, she established her collegiate career as the school’s all-time leader of Rutgers Scarlet Knights with 117 points, 50 goals, and shots.

    Inside Of Carli Lloyd’s Career

    Carli Lloyd started her professional career as a midfielder for the Central Jersey Splash where she scored 11 goals in 1999. Later, she played for New Brunswick Power in 2000. She also played for big clubs including Houston Dash, Manchester City, and others. Currently, she plays for the Sky Blue FC.

    Today, Lloyd is considered as one of the best players in the world. She is highly praised for her outstanding determination, mental strength, and work ethic. Recently, she scored three goals in her first two games of the tournament, one against Thailand and two against Chile. Lloyd became the first player to score in 6 straight Women’s World Cup games.

She also did many endorsement deals with Beats by Dre, Kind, NJM Insurance, Visa, and so on. So, she probably receives some good paychecks from her deals.

    Lloyd was seen on the cover of Howler Magazine and Sports Illustrated. Furthermore, she has modeled for many popular magazines including Glamour, Shape, and so on. She is also featured along with her national teammates in the EA Sports’ game, FIFA 2016. Probably, she might receive a fruitful fortune from her work. She is also a philanthropist and has donated money for Habitat for Humanity.
