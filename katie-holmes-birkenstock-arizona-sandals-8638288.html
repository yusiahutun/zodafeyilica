    Katie Holmes Convinced Me to Trade Spring Shoes for Birkenstock Sandals

    26 Feb 2024, 00:00

    In the middle of winter, just as I was starting to grow tired of the chunky boots I’d started wearing in the fall, Katie Holmes convinced me to ditch them altogether. When she stepped out in a pair of loafers at the start of February, I took it as my sign to do the same and discovered that prematurely moving into spring footwear made my winter wardrobe feel a whole lot fresher. And just last week, she did it again, breaking out a summer staple well ahead of the season.

    When the actress stepped out in New York City, she crafted a spring look that was at the same time both sophisticated and casual. Her button-down top and trousers both had an oversized silhouette that made the otherwise stuffy pieces feel more everyday-appropriate. And by pairing them with classic, all-black Birkenstock sandals, she made the outfit look even more effortlessly cool.

    Arizona BS Leather Sandals

    Birkenstock’s sandals have long been a summer staple, with stars including Reese Witherspoon, Margot Robbie, and Cameron Diaz reaching for the easy, slide-on style, but Holmes proved they’re just as good with breezy spring staples. She slid on a pair of Arizona sandals, which are loved for both their practicality and style and designed with a cork footbed that shapes to the foot, two wide, adjustable straps that keep the shoe in place, and metal buckles. And while her exact style is sold out, there are a number of options from the brand that look almost identical and have hundreds of five-star ratings.

    Arizona Big Buckle Sandals

    The brand’s Big Buckle Arizonas are a customer-favorite, boasting—as the name implies—slightly larger buckles that add a chic edge to the iconic style. Fans rave that the slides have an “elevated look without compromising comfort,” with one person writing that they can’t stop getting compliments on the sandal they initially grabbed for recovery post-surgery. They’re “chic,” “surprisingly soft,” and “so comfortable,” said another shopper. But if you’re looking for something a little more affordable this spring, this all-black plastic version is just $50 (these also happen to be mine and Cameron Diaz’s Arizonas of choice).

    Arizona Waterproof Sandals
