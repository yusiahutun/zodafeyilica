    OnlyFans' popularity has blown up in recent years - and as popular internet creators have started to make the platform their own, so too have athletes.

    2022 has been no exception, and a slew of household names have joined the premium platform over the last 12 months. From Olympians to professional wrestlers, more and more sports stars are putting content behind a paywall.

    And as we welcome 2023 with some New Years' celebrations, Daily Star Sport is having a celebration of its own by looking at six athletes who opened up an OnlyFans page in 2022.

    Ebanie Bridges

    The Blonde Bomber is the latest entry to this list, only starting her OnlyFans account last month - but she's already had fans' jaws on the floor with sultry snaps over Christmas.

    Ebanie Bridges said in a video on Twitter: "I'm so excited to announce that I've joined OnlyFans, so now you can get exclusive content from my training, my everyday life, plus all the fun stuff.

    "Subscribe to my OnlyFans so you can get exclusive content not seen anywhere else, you won't regret it."

    Elise Christie

    Every four years, British speed-skater Elise Christie becomes a household name as she looks to bring home one of Team GB's few Winter Olympic medals.

    However, she also hit headlines in September, when she launched an OnlyFans page, with fans gaining access to the blonde's exclusive content for £11. It was a low-key launch, with Christie just dropping a link in her Instagram caption.

    The 32-year-old shared a series of saucy snaps - with one pic in black lingerie, protecting her modesty by adding teasing emojis and a link to her OnlyFans.

    Paige VanZant

    The queen of MMA launched her long-awaited OnlyFans page in 2022, having previously used other exclusive sites.

    Paige VanZant has expanded her fame away from social media too, popping up in various MMA promotions and professional wrestling company AEW. It was in AEW she was labelled an "OnlyFans chick" by Tay Melo: more on her later.

    Ex-UFC fighter VanZant set the internet on fire in November, when - for Black Friday - she made her OnlyFans free for one-day-only.

    Tay Melo

    Tay Melo started the year as a former Judo star dumped by the WWE, and ended it as one of AEW's rising stars - and, of course, with an OnlyFans page.

    Melo uploaded a snap to her Instagram sitting nude in the bathroom, with just some cheeky emojis to keep it PG, with the caption: "Link in bio. It’s time." The Brazilian is best known for her feud with VanZant in the wrestling promotion.

    However, the pair are yet to meet in the ring one-on-one, perhaps 2023 is the year that could happen.

    Key Alves

    The least known name on this list belongs to Brazilian volleyball libero, Key Alves, who after starting her page in March revealed she's making 50 times her salary on the platform.

    She revealed: "I'm going into my third year on a professional volleyball team and I consider myself a professional athlete, but things off the court started to grow a lot for me. So I started paying a little more attention to that side career.

    "Whether I like it or not, today it's my biggest income. I earn about 50 times more with digital platforms than with volleyball, and more on OnlyFans, because the monthly price of 16 euros is fixed."
