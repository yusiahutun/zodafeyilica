    Alexa Demie was featured in a promotional video for Calvin Klein's Fall 2023 campaign that was released on the brand's official YouTube account on Wednesday.

    The 32-year-old actress cycled through several form-revealing outfits while showing off the fashion house's new items.

    The Euphoria actress, whose late costar Angus Cloud has become the subject of numerous tributes, serves alongside Kendall Jenner as one of the brand's new spokesmodels.

    Demie wore one of the brand's iconic denim jackets during several parts of the video, which was shot in a professional portrait studio and a private residence.

    The Mid90s actress paired her outerwear with a slim-fitting pair of blue jeans during two portions of the shoot, and she showcased her sculpted midsection with a black bra.

    Calvin Klein's global chief marketing officer, Jonathan Bottomley, issued a statement via WMD to express his thoughts about the fashion house's new project.

    'This fall campaign is a celebration of Calvin Klein's most iconic products, featuring brand ambassadors who are shaping the culture of today,' he stated.

    Bottomley then spoke about bringing in new faces to promote the company's new products. 

    'It's the next iteration of our strategy to reshape the image of the brand, reclaiming our core equities of confidence and sensuality, while igniting Calvin's unique ability to create culture,' he said.
