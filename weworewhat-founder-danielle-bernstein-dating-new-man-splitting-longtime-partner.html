    Danielle Bernstein gives glimpses of her new man

    Danielle Bernstein might not be single anymore.

    The WeWoreWhat founder, 31, took to her Instagram to share a snap where she subtly showed off her new possible boyfriend.

    The photo did not show the man’s face but showed the fashion influencer sitting between his legs while wearing black leggings and a gray sweater.

    Her date wore a pair of black pants and white Converse sneakers as they sat on the West side of Manhattan.

    In yet another clip, she walked beside the same man in the same outfit.

    She said in the clip as the man’s feet briefly featured, “This jacket [is] new WeWoreWhat,”

    The fashion designer added, “[It’s] one of my favorites we’ve ever done,”

    Fans noticed the subtle reveal which came less than six months after her split from her fiancier partner, Anthony Adler.

    One internet user commented,

    “She’s so bold to already be talking to her phone about her outfit details while walking with this new man. Not a cringe bone in her body,”

    One fan had something to say about the man’s outfit and wrote,

    “Not the white converse with black socks,”

    Also, Read Split Alert! Danielle Bernstein confirms her breakup with longtime boyfriend Anthony Adler after four years of dating

    Danielle Bernstein broke up with Anthony Adler after 4 years of dating

    Bernstein and Adler ended their relationship after more than four years of dating.

    The This Is Not a Fashion Story author shared a lengthy statement in April saying the breakup was devastating and really painful.

    She wrote on Instagram at the time,

    “I’ve been feeling so anxious to post this, but wanted to address something that I’ve been dealing with privately,”

    Noting that the pair were on different stages, Bernstein added,

    “I’ve recently undergone an unfortunate change in my relationship,”

    “I’ve been grieving letting go of my best friend, the life we had together, and everything I believed would be my future.”

    Danielle also thanked her family and friends for their love and support and told her followers that she was still dealing with the tough breakup.

    She continued,

    “Going through a break up is challenging enough, and I didn’t want to share this update until I felt (sort of) ready to,”

    “I am still going through a whirlwind of emotions and with that, I hope you guys can understand it’s not something I will address further than this right now.”

    The now-exes started dating in January 2019 and lived together in a luxury Soho apartment which even featured in Architectural Digest in 2021 at the time of their breakup.
