    Poplin Maxi Shirtdress

    Though Meghan Markle’s exact black shirt dress is no longer available, you can grab this similar dress for $112. The just-launched maxi style leans a little more casual than Markle’s, with a relaxed fit and side slits that allow for easy movement. Plus, it can be worn as modestly—or not—as you want thanks to the button closure that extends all the way down the front. Personally, I’ll be wearing this all summer; styled more undone, this is great for pool- or beach side-wear and paired with some strappy heeled sandals, it’s also ideal for a dinner out.

    The Straight Jean

    Tan France just told me that straight-leg jeans are the denim style everyone should own this spring, so why not grab this now 53-percent-off pair? The medium-wash jeans have slight fading across the thighs for a vintage-inspired look and a high-waisted fit, with shoppers saying they look great and “fit beautifully.” They’re also incredibly soft, per customers, made of a cotton and elastic blend that “has just enough stretch,” while still allowing the jeans to “maintain their shape.”

    Jennie Boat-Neck Sweater Top

    I’m the first person to get a head-start on summer shopping come April 1, but the reality is that we’re still a solid month away from consistently warm weather (and forever away from workplaces not being freezing). Which is why I’m grabbing this knit top that offers the perfect balance of both: The boat-neck cut gives me that spring-ready look I want, while the lightweight material and long sleeves make it perfect for the office or chilly evenings. One fan called the top “soft” and “flattering,” and per another shopper, this sweater is “classic” and “expensive” looking while being “versatile” enough to be dressed up or worn more casually.
