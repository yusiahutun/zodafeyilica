    The victim in a deadly shooting on Sunday in Davenport has been identified, according to a news release from Davenport Police.

    About 4:15 a.m. Wednesday, Davenport Police, Fire, and Medic EMS responded to the 100 block of East 12th Street in reference to a report of gunfire and a victim. Officers found a man with apparent gunshot wounds who was transported by Medic EMS to a hospital for treatment of serious, life-threatening injuries.

    On Wednesday, the victim – identified as 32-year-old Leonard P. Miller III – died as a result of his injuries, the release says.

    Police say this does not appear to be a random act of violence. They ask that anyone with information about the incident call the Davenport Police Department at 563-326-6125, submit an anonymous tip to QC Crime Stoppers here ,or download the P3 Tips app.

    This incident remains under investigation. No further information was available Friday afternoon.
