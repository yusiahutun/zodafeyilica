    Dlyan and brother Paris recently made history as the first brothers to be named Golden Globe Ambassadors. 

    The sons of the 66 year old Brosnan and filmmaker/environmentalist Keely Shaye Brosnan, 56, were the first brothers ever to serve as Ambassadors.  

    Each year, the Ambassador(s) chosen also align themselves with a worthy cause which is highlighted during the telecast.

    The Brosnan brothers highlighted FEED, a social impact-driven brand founded by Lauren Bush Lauren, delivering nutritious meals to school-aged children around the globe. 

    Previous Ambassadors, which was renamed from Miss Golden Globe in 2017, include Dwayne Johnson's daughter Simone, Jamie Foxx's daughter Corinne and Kelsey Grammer's daughter Greer.
