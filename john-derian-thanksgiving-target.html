    John Derian's Target Collection Is Perfect for Your Friendsgiving

    If you’re anything like me and my friends, the rumblings of Friendsgiving planning has already begun. There’s nothing I love more than a dinner party, and my group of friends loves getting together at someone’s house to celebrate each holiday. Fresh off the heels of my Halloween get-together, John Derian launched a Thanksgiving hosting collection at Target, so yeah, I’m already looking forward to my next event.

    And luckily, this collection won’t break the bank. I’m already eyeing the paper decor that starts at just $10 and manages to add an instant cottage-core, whimsical vibe to any space. There are also nicer, keepsake items that you will reach for in November for years to come. Here’s everything we’re eyeing from this collection.

    • 2-Wick Fall Turkey Jar Candle - Golden Forest Pine Fresh

      I am absolutely obsessed with the vintage vibes this candle has. It’s like pulling a nostalgic element from the ’90s to decorate your own tablescape now that you’re a real adult (or at least trying to be one, anyway).

    • Assorted Fall Turkey Cocktail Glasses (4 pcs)

      This set of four glasses by John Derian looks like something my mom would have spent hundreds of dollars on at Macy’s back in the day. I couldn’t believe that this set was just $15.

    • Assorted Fall Paper Decor Pack 

      This year, my friendsgiving will happen in my friend’s living room with a borrowed table and chairs. What better way to warm up the makeshift dining room than by adding whimsical touches, like the string sign, loose leaves for the table, and more found in this pack?

    • 4pc Assorted Fall Mushroom Appetizer Plates

      Although these plates have a vintage feel, the inclusion of mushrooms make it feel just a bit more modern, since we’ve seen the rise of mushroom-inspired decor over the years. This set of four appetizer plates is just $15.

    •  Quilted Throw Blanket Fall Mums

      The John Derian collection for Target also features so much more than just table decor. This mum throw blanket is the perfect addition to any armchair tucked in the corner of a room. You could even be creative with it and hang it on a wall, drape it over a dining bench, or use it however you like.

    • Turkey Fall Serving Board with Etched Acacia

      Charcuterie is a crucial part of any party or event thrown at home. Make your cheese pairings stand out by serving them on this etched serving board in the shape of a turkey. Hot take? If your Thanksgiving feast is totally vegetarian, honor the turkey in this cruelty-free way.

    • Fall Collage Print 20"x30" Tea Towel Set

      Adding tea towels or kitchen towels is an easy way to warm up any space without having to spend a ton of money, or figure out how to store them in the off-season. Pick up this set of two tea towels that are perfectly inline with the Thanksgiving theme.
