    Do you know what Boho style is? Flowing silhouettes, earthy colour palettes and brass accessories that jingle as you move are all synonymous with the iconic boho style. Most commonly spotted on celebrity fashion icons, festival goers and creatives, this style combines comfortable silhouettes, stunning prints and gorgeous fabrics to give a unique look every time.

    Whether you’re new to this style or are looking for inspiration and new additions to your wardrobe, we’ve handpicked some of our favourite bohemian inspired pieces and shared our tips on how to wear them.

    Table of Contents

    Maxi Dress

    The flowing silhouette and sweeping length of a maxi dress are two elements that are quintessentially bohemian. For a vacation ready look, layer a crochet maxi dress over your favourite bikini or swimsuit – bonus points for finishing your ensemble with beachy waves and tie up gladiator sandals.

    Take your boho look into the night and opt for a maxi dress with paisley or patchwork prints in deep jewel tones, paired with glittering statement jewellery and heeled mules.

    Mini Dress

    Versatile and comfortable, a bohemian inspired mini dress can be styled to perfectly suit all your summer plans. Stay cool in the height of summer with a relaxed fit and length that hems mid thigh. A mini dress with an all over print can be thrown on and immediately give a put together look with minimal effort, or level up your ensemble with a suede fringed bag, leather sandals and gold bangles.

    Long Sleeved Dress

    Ideal for early spring days and cooler evenings, we love the look of a dress that features long sleeves and a mini skirt length. Lean into the bohemian trend and look for blouson sleeves that cuff at your wrist, a shirred or drawstring waistband that cinches your figure and a ruffled collar or tie up neck.

    For the ultimate bohemian look, pair a long sleeved dress with leather western inspired boots and a vest in suede, denim or patchwork.

    Bodycon Dress

    Accentuate your figure with a flattering bodycon style dress. Whether you’re stepping out in the daytime or heading to an after dark event, this style will have you feeling comfortable and confident for whatever occasion. Stay cosy and layer with a long shearling jacket, leather boots and adorn yourself in gold jewels, or enjoy the warmth of the early summer sun on your skin in a dress that features earthy tones, floral prints and a feminine, off the shoulder neckline.

    A trend that has remained popular through the decades, the bohemian style is here to stay. Look for flowing silhouettes, tiered maxi skirts or layered minis and statement blouson sleeves. Finish your boho inspired ensemble with suede and leather accessories and layers of gorgeous gold and bronze jewellery.
