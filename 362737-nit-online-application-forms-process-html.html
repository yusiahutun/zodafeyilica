    How to apply for NIT admission

    Choose a course and ascertain that you have met all the requirements for admission. Your application will be automatically rejected if you fail to upload all the required documents. When you have everything ready, visit the NIT online application platform to process your application. Here is how to apply:

    • Visit NIT's website.
    • Click "Register Now" under "For Online Registration Click Here."
    • You will be directed to the NIT – Online Application System (OAS).
    • Scroll down and click "Start Application."
    • Select your "Application Type."
    • From here, the OAS will guide you through the application process until you fill out the NIT online application forms and upload the required documents.

    The application fee must be paid within four (4) days of completing the application; otherwise, your account will be permanently deleted.

    Since the NIT – Online Application System (OAS) performs a thorough background check on all applications, provide genuine details that match your documents and academic certificates.

    NIT application fee

    NIT's non-refundable application fee is Tshs. 10,000. Masters of Business Administration in Logistics and Transport Management applicants pay an application fee of Tshs. 30,000 (Tanzanians) or $25 (non-Tanzanians). In addition, the applicants of PSV, HGV, Industrial, and VIP courses pay a Tshs. 20,000 pre-test fee and should pass the test before applying for courses.

    Where can you pay NIT fees?

    You can pay fees via mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa and Airtel Money) or send your payment directly to theNIT bank account number 2051100009 at NMB Magomeni Branch after getting a control number. Log into your NIT SIMS account to get your control number.

    How to get an NIT admission letter

    Those who apply through the NIT – Online Application System (OAS) can download their admission letters from the NIT SIMS student information system.

    NIT admission or registration requirements

    Submit the following documents to the Registrar’s office on admission day for you to be registered:

    • Evidence of application, tuition, and other fees payment
    • The letter of invitation to join NIT (admission letter)
    • A duly filled medical examination form
    • Original academic certificates, including a Form IV Certificate and certified academic transcripts (where applicable)
    • Two photocopies of each academic certificate and transcript
    • Ensure your Form IV index Number is the same as in the Form IV Certificate
    • Original birth certificate and two photocopies
    • Caution money
