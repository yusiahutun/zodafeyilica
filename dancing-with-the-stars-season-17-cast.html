    The cast of "Dancing With The Stars" was just revealed, and ABC has gone back to its tired and true formula of casting NFL pros, '90s stars and some reality TV trainwrecks.

    Snooki is perhaps the biggest name on the cast. The former "Jersey Shore" star is in the best shape of her life after giving birth to son Lorenzo last year. She follows in the footsteps of Mike "The Situation" Sorrentino, who competed on Season 11.

    Valerie Harper also made headlines when she joined the show months after announcing she had terminal brain cancer.

    One thing notable about Season 17 is its large number of contestants with dance backgrounds.

    Corbin Bleu has danced extensively in "High School Musical" and other films. And of course, Elizabeth Berkley worked the stage ... and the pole ... in "Showgirls."

    No one could "Dip It Low" like Christina Milian, and Amber Riley did many choreographed numbers on "Glee."

    Who's our money on? Bleu. With Karina Smirnoff as his partner and his heavy background in dance, these two will be one sexy and proficient duo.
