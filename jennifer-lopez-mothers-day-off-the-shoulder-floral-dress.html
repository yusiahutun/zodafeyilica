    Happy Mother’s Day to mothers everywhere—including literal The Mother star Jennifer Lopez!

    For the holiday, the multi-hyphenate was spotted out and about with family in Los Angeles, where she cut a stylish figure in a billowing off-the-shoulder dress. The romantic piece featured a blue-and-white toile de Jouy print, plus a ruffled neckline, puffy mid-length sleeves, and a voluminous tiered maxi skirt. She finished off the look with a pair of strappy white espadrille wedges, an ivory half-moon bag, and amber-tinted aviator sunglasses. She wore her honey brunette hair down in soft waves, and glammed up with some bronze makeup and her go-to glossy nude lip.

    thecelebrityfinder/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    During a recent guest appearance on Live With Kelly and Mark, Lopez opened up about raising her two kids, 15-year-old twins Max and Emme, whom she shares with ex-husband Marc Anthony.

    “It’s crazy, I feel like I had them yesterday. They were just babies a little while ago,” the “Let’s Get Loud” singer said. “It’s the time when they are individuating and they are challenging everything you say and everything you do and everything you are. And that’s what it is. And you have to kind of just ride the waves. I feel like it’s like surfing. I’m just riding the waves—whoops, just got knocked over. Now I’m back! I’m back!”

    She added, “I think it’s particularly hard on moms a little bit. Because they always kind of love Dad. They are always kind of like, ‘Dad’s the best.’ And I was like that with my dad too. My mom told me—and I’ll say it to you right now on TV in front of everybody—I understand you so much more now.”
