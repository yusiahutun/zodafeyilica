    Bebe Rexha received an outpour of love and support from her fans after her boyfriend Keyan Safyari allegedly commented on her weight gain in a screenshot shared by the singer.

    The In the Name of Love music star appeared to have called out her boyfriend Keyan Safyari and seemingly leaked out an alleged text message from him on Instagram. Bebe Rexha has since deleted her original post but Bebe Rexha‘s profile was flooded with messages of love after online users came across the screenshot.

    Bebe Rexha shares alleged text from boyfriend Keyan Safyari

    Bebe Rexha took to her Instagram Stories on Sunday (July 16, 2023) and shared alleged details of personal drama with her boyfriend Keyan Safyari after they started dating in 2020.

    According to the alleged screenshots (as reported by Pop Crave), the Say My Name singer posted an alleged text from her boyfriend commenting on her weight which included a line about the singer’s “35-pound” weight gain.

    The alleged text included a line about the singer’s “changing face” and Keyan reportedly had clarified that the change didn’t mean “that you weren’t beautiful and I never said I didn’t love you”.

    It is not clear if the musician and her boyfriend, a movie maker and owner of a production company, have called it quits. Bebe Rexha hasn’t followed up with a statement or offered an explanation after the deleted screenshot.

    GRV Media and Celebrity Tidbit have reached out to Keyan Safyari for comment.

    View Tweet

    Singer receives an outpour of love and support

    While many fans wait for an explanation from Bebe Rexha and her boyfriend, the singer was told “not to change” as many fans filled her Instagram account with messages of love.

    “You’re beautiful, you’re talented, you have a really cool voice!” one fan commented on Bebe Rexha’s most recent Instagram post read. “You don’t have to change anything about yourself, we love you and send you all the support in the world. Be well, and I love you.”

    “You are so stunning and beautiful woman, so many many many people love you so much,” a second fan added. “You don’t know how powerful you are.”

    Someone else chimed in: “You are light, you are so talented and you are beautiful just the way you are. Your soul is the most beautiful in this world, receive all the true love from the fans.”

    Bebe Rexha on public response after weight gain

    The boyfriend drama comes after Bebe Rexha got candid about the challenges of living in the public eye and constant comments from people about her weight gain and health.

    Speaking on The Jennifer Hudson Show in May, Bebe Rexha explained: “Listen, we’re in the public eye, so that’s bound to happen. I was a lot thinner and I did gain some weight — that comes with the territory. I’m not mad about it because it is true, but when you see things like that, it does mess with you.”

    The Meant to Be singer admitted that the public response to her body image has been “tough” and said that people shouldn’t make certain assumptions about someone’s life.

    “You don’t know what somebody’s going through, what they’re going through in their life, so it kind of is tough,” she added. “But I feel like we’re in 2023 – we should not be talking about people’s weight.”
